What is Spirit Airlines' check-in policy?

Travelers who have reserved Spirit Airlines e-tickets through Faresmatch can use their PNR number to book their chosen seats at online check-in. For Spirit Airlines' economic classes, online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and concludes 12 hours before departure. In addition, travelers can use mobile check-in, which is offered for a small fee, to choose their preferred seat via Web Spirit Airlines check-in. Following mobile check-in, the passenger's registered mail ID will receive a forwarding of the boarding pass and receipt.

One of the main benefits of using Spirit Airlines' online check-in service is that you may check in up to four hours before your flight is scheduled to depart, saving you time. Due to lengthy lines, checking in for a flight usually takes a while. However, online check-in allows you to save time. You only need to drop off your luggage at the specified location and proceed with the required security check after checking in online.

How to check-in online for Spirit Airlines

  • First, click the link for Spirit Airlines' online check-in. This will take you to the official Spirit Airlines flights Check-in website.
  • Enter the necessary information, such as your name, PNR, flight number, etc.
  • Select your seat and make sure you've checked in.
  • A screen displaying your Spirit Airlines boarding card would appear. Spirit Airlines will provide you with an email with the boarding pass, so you may print it now or later.
  • You may check in for your Spirit Airlines flight from home using your laptop by just visiting the official website.

Spirit baggage policy during Check-in

  • When it comes to the baggage policies while flying with Spirit Airlines, the company is very clear. With this airline, you may check a free carry-on bag in addition to your one personal item.
  • If the quantity of luggage exceeds two, there will be a hold baggage or checked baggage fee. Applying TFee to all Overweight and oversized bags is also possible.
  • The type of luggage cost also includes TSports Equipment. Your item should fit above or under the seat in front of you.
  • The carry-on bag measures 50 linear inches (22 x 18 x 10 in) or 127 centimeters (56 x 46 x 25 cm) with handles and wheels.
  • Spirit Airlines has disclosed that the dimensions of the personal item are 40 linear inches (18 x 14 x 8 in) or 102 centimeters (46 x 36 x 20 cm).
  • When making a reservation on Spirit, the carry-on baggage fee is $37.
  • Before online web check-in, it is $47. At the airport counter, it is $55, and at the airport gate, it is $6.

Situated in unincorporated Broward County, about 21 miles north of Miami, in the center of South Florida's Gold Coast, the airport serves the cities of Fort Lauderdale, Hollywood, and Dania Beach.

Orlando International Airport

Six miles southeast of Downtown Orlando, Florida, Orlando International Airport is the main international airport. With 19,618,838 passengers in 2021, it was the busiest airport in the state and the ninth busiest in the country.

Harry Reid International Airport (LAS)

Located in the U.S. state of Nevada, Harry Reid International Airport serves the Las Vegas Valley, a metropolitan region. Situated in the unincorporated region of Paradise, five miles south of downtown Las Vegas, it spans 2,800 acres of land.

BWI Marshall Airport

Thirty miles northeast of Washington, D.C., and nine miles south of Baltimore's downtown are the locations of BWI Marshall Airport, also known as Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport, an international airport in Anne Arundel County, Maryland.
