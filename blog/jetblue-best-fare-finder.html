How to Use JetBlue’s Best Fare Finder?

If you want to get the lowest flight ticket, take a look at how to use the JetBlue Fare Finder:

  • Visit the official JetBlue website (www.jetblue.com). Click on the "Low Fare Calendar" link, which you will find under the "Plan a Trip" section.
  • Now input your travel details. This includes departure city, arrival city, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Using the calendar function, choose the preferred month you want to travel.
  • The JetBlue best fare finder calendar will show you a calendar view of your selected month. Here, you will get to see the lowest available fares for each day, and for your convenience, it is highlighted in blue.
  • The filter option present will help you filter your search based on your travel preferences. This includes the preferred departure and arrival times, number of stops, and cabin class.
  • Click the "Select" button once you have found the ideal flight. Follow the needed steps to book your flight.
  • After you have made your booking, you will be given a confirmation email which states your itinerary details.

FAQs

1. What is the JetBlue Vacation package?

JetBlue Vacations package is a travel package where you will come across roundtrip flights on JetBlue, a hotel and cruise, and a rental car. You will save time and money if you purchase travel services and accommodations through JetBlue Vacations.

2. How to book JetBlue Vacation Package according to FaresMatch?

Booking JetBlue Vacations packages online or over the phone is easy. To book online, you need to visit jetblue.com/vacations or jetblue.com. Visit the Vacations section under the booking tool present at the top of the home page.

3. How do you make changes and cancel fees?

You can make changes by calling at 1-844-528-2229. You can make cancellations either online or by phone. If you fail to cancel the JetBlue Vacations package the day before your scheduled departure, the money accompanying this package will be forfeited.

4. According to FaresMatch, can the travel dates of my JetBlue Vacations package be changed?

Yes, one can make changes to JetBlue Vacations packages. It can be made over the phone by calling JetBlue Vacations at 1-844-528-2229 for assistance.

5. Does the JetBlue vacation package offer tiles?

Yes definitely. One can earn one tile for $100 in their qualifying flight + hotel or flight + cruise package.
