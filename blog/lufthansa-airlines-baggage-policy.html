3). Lufthansa Airlines Carry-On Baggage Policy:

With the help of Lufthansa Airlines Carry-On baggage policy you can understand how much carry-on you can pack for the travel goals as per your travel class. If you want to ensure the hassle-free boarding process then tries to follow the punctual departure and more stowage space as per the carry-on rules. You must follow the dimensions, quantity and weight limited at the departure airport for the carry-on. If the carry-on luggage in Lufthansa Flights is too large or heavy then it will send in the cargo hold for a free and not as the carry-on baggage. In the carry-on policy of the airline a passenger can carry personal item such as medications, purse, bag, or small bag pack. Mobile phones and tablets, power banks or e-cigarettes that contain batteries or rechargeable batteries not included in the checked baggage and only go in the carry-on baggage stuff.

3.1). Carry-On Baggage Dimensions in Lufthansa Airlines:

3.2). Tips for Right Stowage of Lufthansa Carry-On Baggage during Boarding:

A passenger must use the overhead locker for the big items of carry-on baggage such as trolley cases.

You must stow your personal valuables such as laptop bags, mobile phones, handbags and shopping bags in the overhead bin space in front of you.

Passengers can easily hung up the jackets and coats on the hook on the seat in front of them.

One more thing that you need to know here are overhead lockers is only for the emergency exit rows,

3. 3). Items That You Can Carry in the Cabin without Any Charge:

Per child, either a baby carries cot, a child seat or a foldable pushchair.

You can take so-called "pocket buggies" on board with you as part of your permitted carry-on baggage allowance.

Mobility aids, such as wheelchairs and orthopedic aids.

A passenger must check-in the second item of carry-on baggage on particular locations with Lufthansa Regions such as Flights from Italy, India and the USA for the first and business class.

Any kind of personal documents, valuables, mobile phones and laptops should only treated as the carry-on baggage and not as the checked baggage.
