1. Which Terminal Does Southwest Airlines Use at MCO?

The airline operates from Terminal A, Airside 2 at Orlando International Airport (MCO). This terminal is well-equipped with dining, shopping, and essential traveler services, ensuring a comfortable experience for all passengers. Therefore, if you are worried about food and internet connection, there is no need to worry about this because this terminal has all the major amenities.

2. Services & Amenities at Southwest Airlines MCO Terminal

Two significant things to consider when looking at the MCO terminal are check-in and baggage services. This airline provides self-service kiosks and full-service check-in counters at Terminal A, Level 3. On the other hand, passengers can check in online via the website, the mobile app, or the airport. Baggage drop-off is near the Southwest ticket counter, along with Security and TSA PreCheck. The security checkpoints for Terminal A are efficient and include TSA PreCheck lanes for eligible travelers.

To allow enough time for the security check, you must arrive at least 2 hours before a domestic and 3 hours before an international flight.

What are the Food & Dining Options?

Terminal A offers a wide range of food and dining options, including different restaurants and grab-and-go options, including fast food and sit-down dining near Southwest's gates.

Shopping & Duty-Free

If you love shopping, you can explore many big brands at the Southwest Airlines MCO terminal. It has a variety of shops, including gift stores, bookstores, and duty-free shopping for international travellers.

Southwest Airlines Lounge Access

The airline does not operate its lounge, but flyers can access The Club at MCO. This premium lounge is available for purchase or through credit card memberships.

Wi-Fi & Charging Stations

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the Southwest terminal at Orlando Airport, and charging stations near Southwest Airlines' gates allow you to access all these services.

3. Southwest Airlines Arrival & Departure at MCO

Arrivals

Southwest flights arrive at Terminal A, Airside 2.

The baggage claim is located on Level 2 of Terminal A.

Ground transportation, including rental cars, shuttles, taxis, and ride-sharing services, is easily accessible outside the terminal.

Departures

Departing passengers should check in on Level 3 of Terminal A before heading to security.

After security clearance, passengers proceed to Airside 2 to board their flights.

On the other hand, shuttle services and taxis are available, and shuttle and taxi options are available for airport transfers.
