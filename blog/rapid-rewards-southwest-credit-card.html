The airline is known for its cheapest fares, free checked baggage, and customer-friendly approach. Its frequent flyer program is designed to complement these values, providing cardholders with opportunities to earn points for flights, enjoy companion passes, and access travel perks.

Key Benefits of the Rapid Rewards Southwest Credit Card

Generous Sign-Up Bonus

New cardholders often qualify for a substantial sign-up bonus after spending a certain amount within the first three months of account opening. For example, you might earn 50,000 or more rapid rewards, which can be redeemed for flights, hotel stays, and more.

Earn Points on Every Purchase

Yes, this is another thing. You can earn points on every purchase, and cardholders can earn points for every dollar spent. Southwest purchases have higher rates and 3X points per dollar. On the other hand, bonus categories such as dining and travel are also applicable, and standard rates for other everyday purchases are available.

Companion Pass Qualification

Points can also be earned with the Rapid Rewards Southwest Credit Card toward qualifying for the coveted Southwest Companion pass. This pass allows you to bring a companion along on your flights for the cost of fees and Texas.

Annual Travel Perks

Enjoy annual bonus points upon account anniversary.

Travel credits for in-flight purchases or upgraded boarding.

No Blackout Dates

This program is available to redeem points for flights without worrying about blackout dates or seat restrictions.

Different Variants of the Rapid Rewards Credit Card

Southwest offers a few different versions of its Rapid Rewards credit card to suit varying travel and spending habits:

Southwest Rapid Rewards Plus Card

Lower annual fee.

Great for casual travellers.

Includes some travel and purchase protection.

Southwest Rapid Rewards Premier Card

Higher annual fee but more benefits.

No foreign transaction fees.

Additional anniversary bonus points.

Southwest Rapid Rewards Priority Card

Premium version with the highest annual fee.

Includes travel credits, upgraded boarding, and more.

Is the Card Right for You?

The Rapid Rewards Southwest Credit Card is an excellent choice for:

Frequent Southwest flyers who want to earn rewards quickly.

Travellers who value flexibility and no blackout dates.

Those aiming to achieve the Southwest Companion Pass.

However, if you rarely fly Southwest or prefer other airline loyalty programs, this card might not offer the best value.
