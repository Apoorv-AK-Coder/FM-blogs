Bar Harbor

Bar Harbor has a century-long reputation for being a scenic town that combines all the state's best qualities. Bar Harbor is also known as the gateway to Acadia National Park. The town is located along with Frenchman. Hence the travellers get a soothing view both left and right. The craggy coastline looks like a landscape painting, making tourists love the beauty.

The town is the best jumping-off point for some of the whale-watching tours in Maine. The town gives out a special kind of charm which gives a feeling that you are stepping inside the pages of a storybook. Try to taste the local lobster which will be the best thing you have ever tasted. You can take a long stroll, watch the water sway as the day fades and enjoy the tasty local grub. The best time to visit this place is from June to August.

Telluride

The town of Telluride sits in a box canyon of the Colorado Mountains and is best known for skiing. The modern-day village is brimming with all the exciting and cultural things to do. The town is one of the best places on a Low-Cost Airline. Telluride is one of the best places to ski in the world.

To make your skiing experience easier, some trails range from beginners to experts, including four-hike to terrain areas. You can also go hiking in Telluride which sits at 8745 feet above sea level and is surrounded by the San Juan Mountains.

Most of the trails in Telluride lead to waterfalls and lakes, whereas others lead to valleys and run along local creeks. Telluride is very special to hikers and provides the tourist with the most beautiful scenery to entertain during all four seasons.

Try to explore Downtown Telluride which is always humming with shoppers, skiers, hikers, or tourists trying to grab appetizers in between screenings during the Telluride Film Festival. The best months to visit Telluride are from mid-June to August and mid-November to early April.

Jackson Hole

The town has Grand Teton National Park in the North and miles of national forest in every direction. The town has recently shown a rise in tourism. Jackson and Grand Teton boasts unique art and performance venues. The mega ski resorts have transformed the region into a winter wonderland.

The beauty and vastness of Jackson Hole have caught the attention of Hollywood. The town has rugged trails and miles of open space. If you love wildlife, then you must visit the National Elk Refuge. The refuge provides sanctuary to species such as bison, swans, eagles, and others. You will love it. The Snow King Mountain is one of the best places to visit in Jackson Hole.

The place has lots of adventures to partake in. You can go on guided hikes, mountain biking, and gondola rides. If you want to enjoy the scenic view of the town from the top, then take a ride on the Jackson Hole Aerial Tram. Within 10 minutes, the tram ascends 2,700 feet above the mountain valley to the peak. Riders will have a lot of fun.

To have wholesome family fun, you can take your family and children to the Jackson Holes Children Museum. The Museum is a wonderful place to learn, explore and have fun for kids. April, May, September, and October are the best months to visit Jackson Hole.

Monterey

The town's architect is humble, and the lifestyle perfectly synthesizes SoCal laid back and NorCal sophisticated. The northern part of the town attracts most of the tourists. The town is perfect for an ideal road trip stop or a romantic weekend stay. A town is a perfect place for whale watching and home to some of the best California tours.

The town has abundant natural wonders, luxury resorts, and seafood restaurants. Cannery Row is the heart of Monterey tourism and is the best place if you're looking for fun and want to go on a sightseeing trip.

Give a visit to the Monger State Park. The pathway has some of the most interesting buildings and homes, which are significant in the Californian city. The Old fisherman's wharf is one of the most important historical places to visit in this town. The place provides a wide range of seafood restaurants. The Monterey Bay Aquarium has some unique and fascinating marine animals and plants. The best time to visit Monterey is between April and May.

Steamboat Springs

The town is best known for its famous hot springs and is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and people who love adventures. The town is surrounded by working ranches. Fishing and camping are some of the popular activities to do in the town.

The winter months are ideal for snowshoeing and snowmobiling. The town is well known for its skiing activities. The town features 3000 skiable acres, and skiing can be done at an affordable rate and is a prime spot for shredding powder.

To get a panoramic view, you can ride on the Steamboat Gondola. One can hike to the Fish Creek Falls and hit the slopes at Steamboat Ski Resort. You can also bike the Yampa River Core Trail, hop on a zip line, and rest in the Strawberry Park Hot Springs mineral pools. The best months to visit the town are between June and August.
