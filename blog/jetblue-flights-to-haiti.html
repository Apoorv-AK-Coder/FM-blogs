Guides to your Haiti trip

Traveling to Haiti could be the best decision you could have ever made. Traveling to this exotic country with your family during spring break could give you the best memory of your life. The city's landscape and architecture are something to admire and the traditional culture of the people teaches us a lot of lessons in life. JetBlue flights to Haiti are available mostly in the spring breaks as a huge number of people from all around the world visit the country.

Here are the top 3 attractions of Haiti:

Bassin Bleu

This is a natural waterside with hot springs which generate naturally. It is around 55 cm deep and has some caves around it. It is one of the top attractions of the country as people like to explore the entire cave and reach the end to see the waterside for a fun excavation trip.

Historic Citadel

This is a historic site to behold and attracts a huge number of tourists throughout the year. It is said that it was the only house for all the black people during the war, and many people were held captive at this fort. It is situated at the top of the mountain with a beautiful garden surrounding it from four sides.

Papillon Enterprise

It is a magical store that houses many spells and magic potions at the very center of the townhall city. There are many twisted tales regarding the shop and also some artifacts from the early 1800s showcasing the witches.
