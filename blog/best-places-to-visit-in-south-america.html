Blog       10 Best Places to Visit in South America


The continent of South America is full of festivities, beautiful agriculture, and wonderful warm people. Latin America is a much more vibrant place than North America. This continent has wonderful countries such as Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Venezuela, and much more. These countries are known for their vibrant nature and outlook which is loved by all people who visit here. The ecology of these countries is undeniably the most stunning part of it. Exploring South America would be one of the most fun things for you to do and the most endearing experience for you to have.

South America is a culmination of over fourteen countries. That would imply the continent has fourteen different cultures, different races, different rituals, different ecology, and much more. South America will just get more and more interesting as you explore, it. The top ten places you must see and experience when touring through this dynamic continent are as follows –

  1. Angel Falls, Venezuela – This is one of the most magnificent waterfalls for you to explore when you are in South America. Venezuela as a country has many such ecological beauties, although Angela Falls is the most famous among these significant beauties. The rush of the water is so regal you would almost be spellbound by it.
  2. Uyuni Salt Flat, Bolivia- Salt Flats are again one of the most significant ecologies that expand through various countries in South America. Uyuni in Bolivia is the starting point of this large expanse of salt flats which also acts as a breeding area for flamingos. One must explore this ecology wonder when they are in South America without fail.
  3. Los Glaciares National Park, Argentina- Argentina is a significant country in South America. This National park in Argentina showcases vast expanses of glaciers that cover the ends of the earth. From this one place, you can see most of the glaciers that cover the country. This place is perfect for solo travel in South America.
  4. Baby Beach, Aruba- Aruba is one of the most famous seaside locations which is much loved by people all over the world. This beach Aruba is one of the most beautiful beaches that you can experience for free. This is a major public beach where the white sand and the blue waters mesmerize you.
  5. Christ The Redeemer, Brazil- This monument is known as one of the seven wonders of the world. Brazil in general is a country loved by many owing to its fame due to the festivals, and the football team even more. This monument is also one of the reasons why Brazil is so famously loved around the world.
  6. Historical Sanctuary Of Machu Pichu, Peru- One of the most ideal civilizations that has been restored is this historic site. Anyone who has much interest in Ecology would absolutely love this place to begin with. The entirety of the sanctuary is still being uncovered to this date. There is much that you can explore here.
  7. Colca Canyon, Peru- This is one of the most beautiful canyon sceneries that you can explore when you are here in Peru. This canyon has the vast expense of greeneries a hike over this canyon would be a thrilling experience for one to embark on.
  8. Amazon Rainforest, Brazil- We cannot leave out the lush and beautiful Amazon forest when we are speaking about the beauty and must-visit places of South America. Taking a well-guided and extensive tour of this forest will fascinate you to great extents.
  9. Huayna Pichu, Peru- This great and big mountain extends and rises high above the ruins of Machu Pichu. This beautiful mountain is surrounded by an extensive and beautiful river of Urubamba which further elevates the beauty of the mountain.
  10. El Tatio, Chile- The beautiful geothermal geyser fields that add to the ecological diversity of your South American vacation is right here in Chile. One of the most unique experiences in terms of natural phenomena awaits you right here in Chile.

Things To Pack For Your Long Flight

When flying to South America from your country the flight that you will take is going to be pretty long. There are some things that you must pack for such long-haul flights to be comfortable during the long hours of the flight. Some of the essential that you must pack are-

  1. Carry a neck pillow – If you are flying via economy class for such long hours then you must carry a neck pillow. This will help you to avoid a stiff neck and get at least some amount of comfort when you are sleeping.
  2. Carry Some Snacks- You must also carry with you some snacks when you are on these flights. For all those small cravings and snack time don't overpay for inflight snacks.
  3. Carry moisturizer – As you are in high altitude your skin might go very dry. Carrying some simple moisturizers and even hydrating face packs is important for your skin to stay healthy and prevent breakouts during vacation.
  4. Carry Power Banks- There might not be enough plug points to support the charging of your devices. During all those times it is essential you carry a power bank.
