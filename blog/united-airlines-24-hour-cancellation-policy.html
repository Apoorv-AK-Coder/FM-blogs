What is United Airlines 24-Hour Cancellation Policy?

Blog       What is United Airlines 24-Hour Cancellation Policy?


Planning for a trip with the leading airline in the United States, United Airlines, means you must check the flexibility factors of this airline first. This flexible airline makes your travel stress-free because they offer a 24-hour free cancellation policy that allows flyers to modify or cancel their flight tickets without paying any extra fees. Here is the complete guide on United airline's 24-hour cancellation policy.

What Is United Airlines 24-Hour Cancellation Policy?

It enables passengers to cancel or change their flight tickets for United Airlines within 24 hours of booking and get a full refund without any deductions. Hence, if you have specific plans before the trip and your plans may change, buy the refundable tickets only. The United Airlines cancellation policy complies with the U.S. Department of Transportation's regulations, which aim to protect consumers from penalties for quick plan changes.

Key Features of the Policy

Full Refund Eligibility:

Passengers will get a full refund if cancellation is made within 24 hours of the reservation. Hence, if you buy the tickets right now and your travel plans change within 24 hours, claim the full refund within 24 hours. After 24 hours, this policy is not applicable.

No Cancellation Fees:

The airline may also waive the cancellation or change fee within the 24-hour reservation window.

Applies to All Ticket Types:

Now, let's talk about the ticket types under this policy. Well, all ticket types coverers this policy, and basic economy fares are also refundable for the United Airlines 24-hours

The policy covers most ticket types, including non-refundable and basic economy fares. However, the flight must be scheduled to depart at least 7 days (168 hours) from the booking date.

Conditions for the 24-Hour Cancellation Policy

Booking Method:

If you want to entitle the United Airlines ticket refund, as per the policy, the ticket must be booked directly through United Airlines' official website, mobile app, or customer service. If you book the tickets from travel booking portals or third-party portals, you need to check the agent terms.

Timeframe:

Another important factor is the timeframe for claiming a full refund on a United Airlines flight cancellation. The cancellation must occur within 24 hours of the booking confirmation, and the flight departure date must be at least 7 days away from the booking date.

Group Bookings:

Group bookings of United Airlines flights are not eligible to claim a full refund under this policy. You can contact United Airlines customer service to learn more.

How to Cancel Your Flight Within 24 Hours?

United Airlines makes it easy to cancel your flight within the 24-hour window. Follow these steps:

Log In to Your Account:

Visit the United Airlines website or open the mobile app.

Sign in to your MileagePlus account or access your booking using the confirmation number.

Locate Your Booking:

Navigate to the "My Trips" section.

Find the flight you wish to cancel.

Cancel the Flight:

You must select the option and confirm your decision to cancel the United flight.

The airline processed your request and processed a full refund.

Check Your Email:

You will get the refund details in your email.

FAQs

What is the 24-hour cancellation policy?

It’s a simple policy that lets you cancel or change your flight within 24 hours of booking and get a full refund—no fees, no hassles.

Does it work for all ticket types?

Yes! It covers most tickets, including non-refundable and basic economy fares, as long as your flight is at least 7 days away.

Is there a deadline to cancel?

Yes, you need to cancel within 24 hours of booking, and your flight must be at least 7 days from the booking date.

Will I be charged a cancellation fee?

Not at all! United Airlines won’t charge you a penny if you cancel within the 24-hour window.

Can I cancel a group booking?

Unfortunately, group bookings don’t qualify for this policy. For help, contact United Airlines directly.

How can I cancel my flight?

Here’s what to do:

- Log in to your account on United’s website or app.

- Go to "My Trips" and find your flight.

- Select "Cancel" and confirm.

- Check your email for a refund confirmation.

When will I get my refund?

Refunds usually show up in 7 business days for credit cards and up to 20 days for other payment methods.

What if I booked through a travel agent?

You’ll need to check with the travel agency or third-party site about their cancellation rules.

Does this apply to MileagePlus award tickets?

Yes, it does! If you used miles to book, you’re still covered as long as the other conditions are met.

Why is this policy helpful?

It gives you flexibility! If your plans change right after booking, you can cancel stress-free and get your money back.
