The national port

The national harbor port of the city is a major tourist attraction of the country. It is one of the oldest ports in the nation and houses the oldest battleship in the country. The port is quite the tourist attraction for the ship which is now decorated and open for all tourists as a museum for all the guns and tales from the pirates. Economic Counter

Guayaquil is the economic hub of the country. It is the financial sector of the nation and has multiple buildings for such uses. The Economic Counter is the main building where all the financial tasks and money operation is handled. Normal people can't visit the place during office hours for security purposes while every day from 5 pm to 6 pm the place is open for all tourists and normal people.
