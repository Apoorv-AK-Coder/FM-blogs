What is the Companion Pass?

This airline's top-tier reward allows Reward members to designate one person as their companion. This companion can then fly with them for free. However, taxes and fees apply on the booking of any Southwest flights. This companion pass is not just valid for one or a single flight; you can use it for unlimited flights during the validity period.

How to Earn the Companion Pass?

Earning the Companion Pass requires you to either:

Earn 135,000 Rapid Rewards points in a calendar year.

Complete 100 qualifying one-way flights in the same year.

  • Another thing is to fly with Southwest Airlines flight for the major routes from the United States, and you will be able to earn points for every flight you take with this airline.
  • The airline partner also offers other services such as hotel booking, car rental and dining programs to earn extra points.

How to Use the Companion Pass?

  1. First, designate your companion and log in to your Southwest Rapid Rewards Account.
  2. Go to the “My Account” option.
  3. Add the name of the designated companion.
  4. Book your flight as you normally would, using cash or Rapid Rewards points.
