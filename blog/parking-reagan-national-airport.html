On-Airport Parking: Convenience at a Price

On-airport parking facility is quite expensive but not far away from the airport. Therefore, for those who focus on proximity to the terminals, the airport can choose from the different on-site airport parking facilities. These options are ideal for travellers with tight schedules, heavy luggage or those who simply prefer the ease of parking directly at the airport.

Terminal Garages (Terminal 1 and Terminal 2):

This type of parking is best for short-term parking with mobility concerns, and those who are seeking the quickest access to their gates. The rates are also minimal. Parking at Reagan National Airport charges are $6 per hour with a 24-hour maximum of $29. For more accurate prices, you can visit the airport's official website.

Special note on this –

The Cell Phone Lot is currently closed, and the Terminal Garages offer a convenient alternative. One more thing which is essential to know is first 60 minutes of parking are free and allowing enough time to meet arriving passengers. After the first hour, standard hourly rates apply.

What is Economy Lot for Parking?

Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport also has the Economy Lot, which is best for long-term parking and budget-conscious travellers who don’t mind a short shuttle ride. The Economy Lot offers more affordable daily charges, such as $19.

Shuttle Service

There is a complimentary shuttle service that operates 24/7 and runs approximately every 10-15 minutes to and from all terminals. Be sure to factor in extra time for the shuttle when planning your arrival at the airport.

Off-Airport Parking: A Smart Way to Save

Reagan National Airport parking facility is also available off-airport, and this is a quite cost-effective option for the passengers who are looking for affordable choices. These lots, often operated by private companies, offer competitive rates and are typically located just a short distance from the airport.

This option is also best for the extended trips, budget travellers and those who plan. Off-airport parking charges at Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport start from $8 to $15 per day. You can also find that the shuttle service includes a complimentary shuttle service that transports you and your luggage directly to the terminal.

How to Book Parking at Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport?

Don’t miss the pre-booking of Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport Parking because off-airport parking spots must be booked in advance during the peak travel seasons to guarantee a space and lock in the affordable rates. There are many websites where you can find the online parking booking facility of the airports in the United States.
