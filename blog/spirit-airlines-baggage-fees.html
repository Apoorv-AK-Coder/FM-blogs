Baggage fee! It is a big burden on people because they want to travel on a low-cost airline without paying any baggage fees. If your concern is the baggage fee this time, and you want to avoid paying any baggage fee, then Spirit is the right airline for you. There are many benefits to saving baggage fees if you follow the rules and regulations of the airline. Here, you can check the detailed breakdown of Spirit Airlines baggage fees.

The airline is a US-based ultra-low-cost airline, and that's why ticket prices are quite affordable for the basic seat. However, baggage, carry-on, and checked baggage are an additional fee. The airline's model is low-fare, but the additional fees and charges are very high, such as the baggage fee, and as a smart flyer, you must avoid this type of fee while knowing the airline's fee structure.

All about your personal free

It is free. Yes, every passenger is allowed to carry one personal item or one bag, such as a small backpack, purse, laptop bag or small bag. However, you need to follow the dimensions and weight policy for the personal items on Spirit Airlines flights. It must fit under the seat in front of you and follow the airline's size restrictions as well. Your item size should be at most 4X8 inches. If any item exceeds this size, then it will be subject to a carry-on or checked baggage fee. Hence, make sure that you refrain from paying the baggage fee while paying the airline rules.

What are the carry-on baggage fees?

If you want to carry the carry-on luggage extra with your personal item, then you have to pay the carry-on luggage fee, which is around $30 to $65. When you pay your fee while booking the ticket, then it will be low, but if you pay the baggage fee at the airport, then it will be higher. The baggage fee for Spirit Airlines depends on the routes and the particular travel dates. Your carry-on bag must fit in the overhead bin space, and the carry-on bag size requirement is 22 x 18 x 10 inches.

Know About Checked Baggage Fees

On the other hand, checked baggage fees vary according to the number of bags. For example, the cost for the first checked bag is $20 to $25, for the second checked bag, it is $45 to $60, and for the additional checked bag, it is $85. Hence, smart packers always avoid baggage fees while travelling with a low-cost airline.

What do you need to pay Spirit Airlines Overweight and Oversized Fees?

If your bags on Spirit Airlines flights are overweight or oversized, then you need to pay the overweight and oversized bag fee, which is -

- Overweight fees: $79 for bags 41–50 lbs, $125 for 51–100 lbs.

- Oversized fees: $150 for bags exceeding 62 linear inches.

People who want to avoid this fee must weigh and size their bags at home before leaving for the airport.

Early Purchase = Big Savings

Baggage must be purchased early, while booking tickets, or during check-in because it helps you earn big savings. The fee will increase if you pay it closer to your departure date or at the gate.

Get the Spirit Saver$ Club Perks

You can also join the Spirit Saver Club to reduce the baggage fee. Yes, members can avail of a discount on the baggage fee as they are frequent flyer program members of the airline. Free Spirit is the airline's frequent flyer program.

Pack Smartly to avoid the baggage fee

Last but not least, you must pack smartly and efficiently if you want to avoid the baggage fee or do not want to pay the baggage fee at the gate. Frequent travelers of the airline understand the baggage policy of Spirit Airlines, and that's why they carry the bags according to the airline's rules and regulations. This is a low-cost airline for cheap flight tickets; if you are paying the extra baggage fee, then savings are not possible.

FAQs

Does Spirit Airlines allow a free personal item?

Yes, Spirit Airlines allows each passenger to carry one free personal item, such as a small backpack, purse, or laptop bag. However, it must adhere to the size restrictions (18 x 14 x 8 inches) and fit under the seat in front of you.

How much does Spirit Airlines charge for carry-on baggage?

Carry-on baggage fees range from $30 to $65, depending on when you pay (cheaper during booking and more expensive at the airport). Carry-on bags must meet the size requirement of 22 x 18 x 10 inches and fit in the overhead bin.

What are the fees for checked baggage on Spirit Airlines?

Checked baggage fees vary by the number of bags:

- First checked bag: $20–$25

- Second checked bag: $45–$60

- Additional checked bags: $85+

Weigh your bags ahead of time to avoid excess charges.

What is Spirit Airlines' overweight baggage policy?

If your checked bag exceeds Spirit’s weight limits, here’s what you’ll pay:

- 41–50 lbs: $79

- 51–100 lbs: $125

Are there fees for oversized bags?

Yes, oversized baggage (exceeding 62 linear inches) incurs a fee of $150. It’s essential to measure your bags to avoid these additional charges.

How can I save on Spirit Airlines baggage fees?

You can save on baggage fees by:

- Paying early: Purchase baggage when booking or during online check-in for the lowest rates.

- Joining the Spirit Saver$ Club: Members receive discounts on baggage fees.

- Packing smartly: Minimize luggage to avoid overweight or oversized charges.

What is the Spirit Saver$ Club, and how does it help with baggage fees?

The Spirit Saver$ Club is a membership program offering discounts on baggage fees, ticket prices, and other perks. It’s ideal for frequent flyers looking to save on additional costs.

How can I avoid Spirit Airlines baggage fees altogether?

To avoid baggage fees:

- Stick to the free personal item size limit.

- Pack efficiently to fit everything into a single carry-on or checked bag.

- Pre-plan and weigh your luggage to avoid overweight fees.

Why are baggage fees higher at the airport?

Spirit Airlines increases baggage fees closer to departure to encourage early purchases and efficient packing. Paying during booking or online check-in ensures lower fees.

What should I do to pack smartly for Spirit Airlines flights?

To pack smartly:

- Use compression bags to fit more into smaller luggage.

- Share luggage space with travel companions.

- Avoid packing unnecessary items to stay within weight and size limits.

Is Spirit Airlines a good option for budget travelers?

Yes, Spirit Airlines is an excellent option for budget travelers, provided they follow baggage rules and avoid unnecessary fees. Early planning and packing efficiently can significantly enhance savings.
