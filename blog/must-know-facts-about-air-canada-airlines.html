Blog       Must Know Facts About Air Canada Airlines


As a flyer of Air Canada you must know the major facts about the airline. The airline based out Canada and the largest airline of the country. Thus, flying with this airline means you can add more and more amazing memories in your travel. We are come with the Must Know Facts About Air Canada Airlines.

#1. Founded in 1937:

One fact that every person must know about the Air Canada Airline is founded year of the airline. Well, 1937 is the founded year of the airline and that’s why this airline also comes in the list of oldest airline of the Canada.

#2. Aeroplan is Frequent Flyer Program:

The second must know facts is about the frequent flyer program of the airline. Aeroplan is the FFP or miles earning program of the flag carrier. You can also spend the miles and rewards on the different travel tasks.

#3. Total 222 Destination of Air Canada Airlines:

The total destinations of Air Canada Airlines, are 222. The extensive network of the airline serves across the world and Fleet Count of the airline is 166.

#4. Member of Star Alliance:

One more thing that you can’t miss is Air Canada Airlines. For the Air Canada Airlines Reservations you will get the advantages of Star Alliance for the holiday purpose.

#5. Cabin Class of Air Canada Airlines:

Cabin Classes of Air Canada Airlines are also quite different from other airlines. Signature Class, North American Business Class, Premium Economy Class and Economy Class are the cabin classes of the Air Canada.
