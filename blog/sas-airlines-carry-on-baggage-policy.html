Blog       SAS Airlines Carry-On Baggage Policy


Baggage policy of the SAS Airlines Flights is very much important for you. We are going to brief a small section about SAS Airlines Carry-On Baggage Policy for the passengers. Take a look on the complete overview about the baggage policy of SAS Airlines. You can bring one carry-on bag and one personal item (like your small handbag or laptop in the cabin) without any costing. The baggage fee of SAS Airline is mainly depending on the type of ticket that you are going to purchase from the SAS Airlines Website. You must follow the weight and size limits about the carry-on baggage. The bags must fit under the seat in front of you.

SAS Airlines Carry-On Bag Weight Requirements with Number of Bags:

  • SAS Go 1 x 8 kg
  • SAS Plus 1 x 8 kg
  • SAS Plus 2 x 8 kg (to/from Asia and the US)
  • SAS Business 2 x 8 kg
  • Maximum size of any piece of carry-on bag: 55 cm x 40 cm x 23 cm (length x width x depth).
  • Maximum size of any hand bag or laptop: 40 cm x 30 cm x 15 cm (length x width x depth).

Baggage Policy Instruction about Fragile and Valuable Items:

If you are carrying valuable or fragile items for the travel with SAS Airlines Flights such as passports, medication, laptop, money, camera, devices, or Jewellery then pack smaller these items or invidiously. SAS Airline not allowed larger items in the cabin such as computer or televisions in the cabin and these items will go in the cargo. You can find SAS Cheap Flights at fares match.
