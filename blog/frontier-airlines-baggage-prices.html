Frontier Airlines Baggage Allowances

There are different types of baggage fees with this airline. You can bring different types of baggage to the plane; some baggage is free, and some have charges. It is important to understand the difference between personal items, carry-on bags, and checked luggage, so you know what to expect.

Personal Items

One personal item is free per passenger in the baggage policy of Frontier airlines tickets. However, the item must fit under the seat in front of you, and it must be a small bag such as a backpack, purse, laptop bag, or briefcase.

The maximum dimensions size limit for a personal item are 8 inches high, 14 inches wide, and 18 inches deep. If your personal item exceeds these dimensions, it will be considered a carry-on and subject to additional fees.

Carry-On Bags

The carry-on bag fee is applicable and this item is that which is stored in the overhead bin. The fee for carrying luggage is applied when you purchase the extra baggage during the booking or add it to your flight prior to departure.

The carry-on bag size limit must be within 10 inches high, 16 inches wide, and 24 inches deep. The weight limit for a carry-on bag is 35 pounds.

What is the price of carry-on bag?

The price of carry bags typically ranges from $39 to $64, depending on when you purchase them. Purchasing an extra bag at least 24 hours before departure will save you from paying higher fees at the airport.

Checked Bags

If you are thinking of checking a bag, then you must pay the Frontier Airlines baggage prices on the checked bag. The checked bag fee is usually higher than the carry-on bag. The size limit for Frontier checked bag is 62 inches in total linear dimensions (length + width + height). The maximum weight limit for a checked bag is 50 pounds.

Checked baggage prices in Frontier

It is between $39 to $99 each way, and you have to pay for the baggage allowance in advance for the cost-effective option.

Additional Fees for Overweight or Oversized Bags

Yes, there is a fee for overweight and oversized bags in Frontier Airlines. Frontier airlines baggage prices are applicable in the different categories, and it is one of the categories. Overweight bags weighing more than 50 pounds but less than 100 pounds will incur an overweight charge of $75. If a checked bag weighs over 100 pounds, it will not be accepted for travel. For oversize bags that exceed more than the maximum size of 62 linear inches, the oversized baggage fee is $75.

Tips for Avoiding Extra Baggage Fees

However, here we can say that you can add the Frontier baggage fee and prices quickly, but there are some ways through which you can save on the baggage fee of the airline, and here are the ways.

You must pack light

As per the personal item policy of the airline, the personal item is free in the baggage policy. Hence, you must pack light and carry one small bag for free. People who pack light and stick to only the essentials always avoid the extra fee on the carry-on and checked bag. A compact and small suitcase is always the right choice while traveling with this low-cost airline.

Pre-Purchase Your Baggage Allowance

Suppose you pre-purchase your baggage allowance or add your baggage during the time of booking or a flight ticket purchase or at least 24 hours before your flight to save money. This is the right move for buying your baggage allowance in advance because baggage allowance at the airport is very expensive.

Do you know about Bundle Bags?

Yes, these bags with the “The Works” package include a combination of benefits such as one checked bag, one carry-on bag, seat selection, and priority boarding.

Frequent Flyers: Consider Frontier Miles

On the other hand, if you are a member of the frequent flyer program of the airline Frontier Miles, then you can also purchase the baggage while using the miles of the program. You can use your rewards and points to upgrade the ticket, purchase food, or pay the baggage fee as well.
