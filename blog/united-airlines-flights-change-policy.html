Flight Change Policy of United Airlines

United Airlines prioritises the ease of its customers in terms of reserving, changing and cancelling flight tickets. The airlines focus on streamlining and simplifying complex processes that might have taken up more time, effort and paperwork. All of this is done to ensure that the processes run smoothly and efficiently.

There may exist several personal and professional reasons for travellers to change United Airlines flights. Some common reasons why travellers may opt to change their flights are included in the list below :

  • Sometimes, travellers may be forced to change flights due to the passing away of a beloved and close family member or their intended travel partner. Such a problem means that the traveller is eligible for a bereavement waiver.
  • Another reason for changing flights may be an illness which affects them, their loved one, or their travel partner, resulting in the need for urgent medical care. Such a problem means that the traveller for a medical waiver.
  • Sometimes, however, travellers may opt to change flights for business or personal reasons. These are usually not considered under any waiver

How to Change United Airlines Flights?

Travellers need to follow a few simple steps to change a United Airlines flight to opt for a new destination or a new time. These are listed below:

  • Open your laptop, desktop or mobile personal device and ensure that it is properly charged and well connected to the internet.
  • Type United Airlines flight change in the search bar of whatever search engine is installed on your device.
  • Go to the United Airlines official website that will appear and click on the My Trips tab. Select the Change Flights option there.
  • Select the Edit option to make changes to your flight. Choose the new flight you wish to opt for.

The process will be complete as long as both the flights are operated by United Airlines or United Express, the arrival and departure airports are unchanged and the new flight leaves within twenty-four hours before or after the departure of your previously chosen flight.
