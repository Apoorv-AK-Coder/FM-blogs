Blog       8 Romantic Getaways for Couples


Time spent with a loved one is always a cherished part of our lives. It becomes even more special if you can spend it surrounded by the beauty of nature in a location far away from the routine and monotony of daily life, where all you need to focus on is your beloved and their company and not the more mundane struggles of life and work.

Top destinations for all travellers

Some of the best destinations that travellers looking for romantic getaways should make a point to visit are included in the list given below:

Scotland: The country located in the British Isles of the United Kingdom is a fantastic location for couples who love the mountains with a stop at the famous Gretna Green for tradition.

Chassignolles, France: The quiet commune of France has a lot to offer to any traveller seeking a change of pace after the hustle and bustle of daily life and gives couples a special chance to enjoy each other’s company.

British Virgin Islands: The British Virgin Islands are a part of the British Overseas Territories forming the United Kingdom and a favourite for couples seeking beach vacations. The Terrence B. Lettsome International Airport, once known to travellers as the Beef Island Airport, provides service to travellers travelling to and from the region from its location on the Beef Island linked to the larger Tortola Island by the Queen Elizabeth the Second Bridge and further to the U. S. Virgin Islands.

Montana, United States of America: The state of Montana in the United States of America offers travellers a rugged lifestyle among the mountains in the Mountain Western part of the country.

Tuscany, Italy: Tuscany is a popular region in Italy famous for its art and architecture which allows visiting couples to escape to beautiful scenes all around them.

Zihuatanejo, Mexico: The city of Zihuatanejo in Mexico is a coastal resort city known among travellers as the perfect spot for a stay beside the sea.

Palm Springs, California: The Palm Springs city in the California State of the United States of America is the location that couples visit to spend some time surrounded by the desert.

Whidbey Island, Washington: Whidbey Island of Washington is another popular couples destination in the United States of America with wooded forestland and sandy beaches.

How can You enjoy your flights?

Travellers getting on flights to head for romantic getaways could try certain things to get more enjoyment out of what otherwise threatens to be a boring journey such as a special playlist curated for the taste of both partners or a special movie that they will both enjoy loaded on their laptop to transform the journey into bonding time between two individuals enjoying each other’s company.

Best time to go on trips

For couples, the best time to go on trips is the low season of their destination when the crowd at these sites has abated allowing them to soak in the presence of their partner. Special days like Valentine’s Day and White Day are often the most guaranteed time for special discounts for travelling couples making these days a joint celebration for their love and wandering spirit.

FAQs

What is the nearest airport to Zihuatanejo Mexico?

The nearest airport to Zihuatanejo is the Ixtapa Zihuatanejo International Airport which is located in the Guerrero state of Mexico and serves over six hundred and fifty-four thousand passengers travelling to and from the cities of Ixtapa and Zihuatanejo in the country.

What is the nearest airport to Whidbey Island of Washington?

The nearest airport to Whidbey Island of Washington is the DeLaurentis Airport at Oak Harbour, once known to travellers as the A. J. Eisenberg Airport and the Wes Lupien Airport, which is located at a distance of six kilometres to the southwestern direction of the central business district of the city of Oak Harbour.

What is the nearest airport to Palm Springs in California?

The nearest airport to Palm Springs in California is the Palm Springs International Airport which provides service to over three million passengers travelling to and from the Palm Springs area in the Coachella Valley in the Inland Empire of the United States of America.

What is the most important airport in Montana?

The most important airport in Montana is the Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport which is located in Belgrade city of Montana State of the United States of America at a distance of eight miles or thirteen kilometres from Bozeman city and provides service to over two million passengers travelling to and from the state every year.

What is the most important airport of Scotland?

The most important airport of Scotland is the Edinburgh Airport located in the Ingliston region of the Edinburgh city providing service to over fourteen million passengers travelling to and from the country every year.
