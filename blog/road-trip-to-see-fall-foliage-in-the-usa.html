Routes Various, Michigan

The Upper Peninsula of Michigan is the place to go in the fall, with some of the most spectacular autumn scenery in the country, including multiple waterfalls. Highway 20, Washington

Budget travel Highway 20 in Washington State winds through the rugged North Cascades, providing unrivalled panoramic beauty that is accentuated by the advent of autumn's colours. Columbia Gorge, Oregon

The Columbia Gorge, which flows through the Cascades for 80 miles, acts as a natural border between Washington and Oregon. It will steal your breath away in the fall when the firs, cottonwoods, big-leaf maples, Oregon ash, and twisted pines display their stunning colours. You may stop for a taste at one of the many wineries along the way, hike the miles of trails, many of which lead to waterfalls, or take a raft or kayak out on the river.
