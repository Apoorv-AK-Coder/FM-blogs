Blog       Essential Tips for Parents Travelling with a Baby


Travellers who have become parents recently often face the dilemma of either taking their young child along with them on what might be a difficult journey in a location unknown to their child or leaving their child behind for the whole duration of the journey, this article aims to make the first option easier and more viable for parents by offering ideas on how to travel with a baby.

Things You Should Keep in Mind While travelling with babies

Travel with babies may or may not be a voluntary decision on the part of the parent due to various economic, social, circumstantial or even medical reasons. The tips included in the list given below help travellers headed for family travel with young children:

  1. Travellers should start factoring in the addition of their baby from the initial stages of the journey which means making reservations before their journey to the location for vehicles and before their arrival for accommodation at hotels, resorts and room stays to prevent any delays or lengthy waiting processes.
  2. Travellers should book airlines, hotels and other services which will be accommodating to young children. Travellers should look up the policies of businesses prior to booking their services to ensure their compatibility and carefully look through any rules listed on the websites or guidelines provided by the establishments.
  3. Travellers should make sure to carry all necessities that their child may need such as medication and hygiene kits which might be unavailable at the location you intend to visit. Sometimes travellers may also need to carry specific objects from brands which are particularly suitable for their children.
  4. Parents should make sure to feed their babies at take-off and departure to make sure that their ears align with the change in pressure as babies can be particularly sensitive to that. Travellers can also consider a pacifier for this purpose if that is more suitable and fits in better with the feeding schedule of the child.
  5. Parents know the routines of their babies and it is best to stick to them as much as possible due to difficulties faced by young children while adjusting to changes in their surroundings. Having the familiarity of the same feeding and napping times makes the process significantly more comfortable for babies.

Necessary Objects to Carry to the trip

  • Objects that parents of babies need to carry with themselves on a family holiday must start with the mention of baby food which might not be available at the location.
  • Some babies are particularly sensitive to food items from certain brands and care must be taken to avoid introducing babies to new food throughout the duration of the trip.
  • Travellers next need to carry space-saving luggage options such as package cubes to accommodate the extra luggage that is inevitable when babies are involved in the journey.
  • Travellers should also try to bring items familiar to the baby such as their favourite toy or blanket which will provide comfort to the baby as they adjust to their new surroundings.
  • Other items that parents will find integral to the journey are things like extra diapers which will allow children to be comfortable and extra clothes for the consistent ability of children to get their clothes soiled by various means.
  • Travellers should also remember to bring a cover in case they intend to breastfeed the baby during the journey.

Suitable vehicles for babies

When travellers with young children head out on a family vacation, it is advisable to use a personal vehicle or one rented from a company that has child-friendly policies. Travellers should make sure to check the expiry dates of seats on vehicles in both cases and check the reinforcements keeping baby seats in place before the journey.

Travellers might not always be able to use private vehicles for a journey and need to rely on public transportation where the best strategy is to make sure the baby is asleep during the journey. Travellers should make sure to dress babies in their most comfortable clothes for the duration of the journey especially cotton and linen clothes that allow their skin to breathe.

On flights, travellers can choose to opt for reserving early boarding if they feel that will make it easier to set up their space or opt to board later after the child has tired themselves out at the airport. For travellers intending to use flights for travel, their aim should be to reserve an aisle seat which will allow them to visit the washroom to change the diaper of the baby as many times as necessary.

An Assistance for the babies

Young parents may need help to handle their young children due to the diverse needs of babies and while this is especially true for first-time parents who may not have any prior experience, it applies to most people. In such situations, it is a good idea to bring other family members on the trip who can be trusted to take care of the baby providing a brief respite to the parents.

Conclusion

Travelling with a baby is bound to be stressful for every new parent and fraught with a lot of worry about whether the journey will go as planned or not. The best strategy in these situations is to expect the unexpected and deal with situations as they arise without letting them dictate the journey or interfere with its enjoyment.
