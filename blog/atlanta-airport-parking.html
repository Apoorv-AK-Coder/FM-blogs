Atlanta Airport Parking Guide, Map, Charges, and More

Blog       Atlanta Airport Parking Guide, Map, Charges, and More


One of the major airports in the United States, also known as the busiest airport, is Atlanta International Airport. Airport parking facilities are also very high-end, and on-airport parking options are available. For long-term and daily parking, $30/day (up from $19) charges are applicable. There is a wide range of parking options available at the Atlanta airport, both on-site and off-site. Charges are different and accommodate different needs and budgets. It is the leading and biggest airport, and that’s why parking demand is always high. Here is a breakdown of standard parking options and considerations.

On-Airport Parking

On-site parking is a much safer and more accessible option at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. This type of parking is available for both domestic and international terminals. Therefore, it is more convenient but can be expensive. Hourly parking charges are different for the North and South Hourly Lots and the International Hourly Lot. However, this type of parking is best for short stays, picking up/dropping off passengers. For the first hour, parking charges are around $3 to $10, and rates have recently increased. The parking cost at Atlanta International Airport may increase and range from $36 to $100, depending on the particular lot and recent rate changes. Economy parking lots are North, South, and West Economy Lots and are best for long-term parking, a more budget-friendly on-site option. These lots also often have shuttle service to the terminals.

ATL West Deck:

This is an ideal option for all those people who are looking for secure and covered parking that is connected to the terminal via the SkyTrain. The rates for this parking lot are around $16 - $30 per day.

ATL Select:

ATL Select parking offers flexible options, including uncovered, covered, and oversized vehicle parking. The rates are approximately $10 - $15 per day. If you choose, the covered parking is around $14 - $20 per day, and oversized, around $28 - $40 per day. Shuttle services are also available from the ATL Select.

Park-Ride Lots (A and C, International Park-Ride):

Best for: Most budget-friendly on-site long-term parking.

Rates (approximate): Around $10 - $15 per day for domestic, $14 - $30 for international.

Includes complimentary shuttle services to terminals.

Off-Airport Parking

On the other hand, you can also choose off-airport parking, and many third-party parking providers and hotels near ATL offer off-site parking with shuttle services. These can often be significantly cheaper than on-site options, especially for long-term parking. This parking offers the lowest rates, usually starting from $4 to $7 per day. The shuttle service provides free, frequent shuttles to and from the airport terminals (domestic and/or international).

When we talk about the amenities, we can say that additional services like car washes, EV charging, luggage assistance, and even pet boarding are available. Reservations are essential and highly recommended to guarantee a spot and often get better rates.
