Checking Southwest Airlines Flight Status

It is important to always keep track of the flight you choose to fly. To avoid missing your flight, you should keep your flight status updated. You can easily check your Southwest Airlines flight status by following these steps:

  1. Open the browser on your device of preference.
  2. Search for "Southwest Flights Status" through your browser's search bar.
  3. Click on "Check Flight Status" on the airline's website.
  4. Enter the departure place, your destination city, and the date of travel.
  5. You will be shown the proper flight status of this airline, allowing you to adjust your schedule accordingly.
