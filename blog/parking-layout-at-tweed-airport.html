Well, the Parking at Tweed New Haven Airport (HVN) is directly located across the terminal building, and you can reach the parking on a short walk from your car to the check-in counters. The airport maintains one primary on-site parking area that is divided into two distinct lots to accommodate different travel durations.

Short-Term and Long-Term Parking Lots

Short-Term Parking Lot: This lot is situated closest to the terminal entrance and is designed for quick trips, as well as for dropping off or picking up passengers.

Long-Term Parking Lot: This parking lot is located just beyond the short-term lot, and this area offers a more affordable option for daily parking for travelers leaving their vehicles for one day or longer.

Tweed Airport Parking Rates

Here is the list of parking rates at Tweed Airport, and these are the recent and updates rates.

Hourly Rate: $3.00 per hour

Short-Term Lot Daily Maximum: $20.00 per 24-hour period

Long-Term Lot Daily Maximum: $15.00 per 24-hour period

Payment Methods: The exit plazas accept all major credit cards for payment.

Critical Information: Parking Lot Capacity

Usually, the demand for parking is always high at this airport, and that’s why if you are planning the parking at this airport then you must arrive early. The parking lots at HVN frequently reach full capacity, especially during peak travel times such as holidays and weekends. There is no reservation system at this airport, and it currently does not offer a pre-booking or reservation system for parking. All spots are available on a first-come and first-served basis. The official recommendation is due to the limited space, the airport strongly advises passengers to consider being dropped off or using ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft or local taxis.

Accessible Parking

Handicap-accessible parking spaces are available in the Short-Term parking lot, and this is the most convenient access to the terminal for individuals with disabilities.

FAQs

1. How much is parking at Tweed New Haven Airport?

The parking charges are Tweed Airport are $3 per hour, and the maximum daily Rate per hour is $20 for the short-term lot and $15 for the long-term lot.

2. Is there a shuttle from the parking lots at Tweed Airport?

No, a shuttle service is not necessary, and all official on-site parking lots are within a short, easy walking distance of the airport terminal.

3. What happens if the parking lots are full?

You will not be able to park at the airport if the on-site parking is full, and that’s why it is essential to have a backup plan or check all the nearest dropped off or use a taxi or ride-sharing service.

4. Can I reserve parking at Tweed Airport in advance?

No, you cannot currently reserve a parking spot in advance at Tweed New Haven Airport.

5. Is there a weekly parking rate?

There is no special discounted weekly rate. The cost for a week would be calculated by multiplying the daily maximum Rate by the number of days. For example, a 7-day trip in the Long-Term lot would cost $105 ($15 x 7).
