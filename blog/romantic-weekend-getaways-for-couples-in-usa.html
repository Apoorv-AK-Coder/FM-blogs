Following are the 17 best getaway spots for couples to make a flight booking for romantic weekend in the USA:

Sonoma, California

Sonoma is a beautiful place to visit. This place has it all: farms, vineyards, and a general sense of well-being. You should book a cedar bath for two at Osmosis day spa after hopping between vineyards and indulging in some local juice and fun (don't miss the pool at Francis Ford Coppola or the thriving acres of spring tulips at Ferrari-Carano).

Glacier National Park, Montana

This park warrants one of the must-visit getaway spots on your bucket list, with mountain lakes surrounded by rugged peaks, 600-foot waterfalls, and glaciers. Hike, horseback ride, or take a boat ride to see if you can spot a grizzly bear or mountain goat, then warm yourself next to a fire or a big supper.

Kauai, Hawaii

If craggy cliffs and sunny sand, emerald hills, and epic hikes are your ideas of paradise, plan a trip to Kauai with your better half. Take a helicopter trip past the Jurassic Park waterfall, kayak Hawaii's only navigable river, or wander the untamed Na Pali Coast on the "Garden Isle," a green utopia.

Woodstock, Vermont

With its white-steepled churches and snug cabins complete with a fireplace, Woodstock offers the ideal lovely New England experience, replete with sipping red wine, terrific skiing, and lots of cuddling with the one you love. So do the airlines bookings today for Woodstock.

New York

New York romance is all about connecting with your lover amid the craziness, thanks to the city's constant hustle. Rent a rowboat in Central Park or, throw caution to the wind and visit the Museum of Sex or take the tram to Roosevelt Island for panoramic views of the city. So many amazing things to experience in one place. Isn't it?

Block Island, Rhode

The Narragansett tribe named this tiny isle "Island of the Little God," and it is a world away from the mainland, 12 miles off the coast of Rhode Island. Rent a bike or moped, order a sandwich from Three Sisters, then head out to your favorite beach or scenic trail with your spouse.

Amelia Island, Florida

This Atlantic Coast barrier island, just inside the Florida line, is ideal for making the flight booking for romantic weekend getaway spots or a low-key getaway. Book a stay at a historic B&B or at the Ritz-Carlton, where you may sleep on a bed overlooking the beach. Then spend your days lazing in the sun while horseback riding along the beach.

Taos, New Mexico

Taos is a refuge for artists, with a plethora of galleries featuring both classic and contemporary fine art. There are also day spas, world-class eating options, and the opportunity to enjoy a romantic hot air balloon flight over the beautiful scenery. San Juan Islands, Washington

Lighthouse walks, coastal kayaking, alpaca or lavender farms excursions, and fresh seafood are all part of island life. Book a summer whale-watching excursion to see one of three nearby orca pods, and then retire to the Bird Rock Hotel if you get its booking, where loaner bikes allow you to explore at your leisure.

Fairbanks, Alaska

Seeing the Northern Lights with your significant other can be a very romantic and memorable experience. Guests can choose to stay at Chena Hot Springs Resort in Fairbanks, Alaska may enjoy a stunning light show created by the relaxing hot springs. Dog sled rides, massage, and ice fishing are among the other activities available.

Cannon Beach, Oregon

Cannon Beach, on Oregon's magnificent northern coast, is noted for its spectacular winter storms, which allow visitors to hear the crashing waves on the rocky shore. Look for grey whales passing right off the coast between mid-December and mid-January, or simply relax in a comfortable room with a bottle of fine wine and enjoy the spectacular weather. Romantic candle lit meals by the water, a couple's massage, and a soothing bath in a hot tub. You and your partner are going to love it.

Yellowstone National Park, Wyoming

Couples can enjoy privacy and tranquility in this 2.2 million-acre park. Discover some of the world's most strange and beautiful places, where animals outnumber humans. Cuddle up in a richly quilted bed at the historic Old Faithful Snow Lodge in the evening, then spend the day together snowshoeing or cross-country skiing on the park's kilometer-long routes, which pass vibrantly colored mineral springs, frozen waterfalls, and erupting geysers are all included best deals for couples in this park.

Estes Park, Colorado

Estes Park, Colorado, is a picturesque town with romantic hotels and magnificent mountain-style cabins with luxury facilities such as claw-footed tubs or in-room Jacuzzis, toasty fireplaces, and even hot tubs overlooking a quiet river or the park. This is indeed one of the best getaway spots in the US for couples.

Amelia Island, Florida

A romantic daytime coastal horseback ride for two is ideal. On a 40-foot yacht, a private sunset cruise? For you and your amour, Amelia Island, a barrier island off Florida's Atlantic coast, has it all. Exploring the lovely Amelia Island Trail, which leads to Amelia Island State Park's acres of sandy beaches, salt marshes, and maritime forests, is a must-do. After a day of sightseeing, unwind with a couples massage at one of the island's high-end resorts.

Charleston, SC

Charleston, South Carolina, is a laid-back and of the best getaway spots with historic homes, art galleries, and delectable Southern cuisine (perfect for foodie couples), as well as sunning beaches nearby. To add to the romance, book a sunset harbor tour for just the two of you. There is something for everyone, from beach resorts to charming inns. For budget-conscious couples looking to avoid the high expenses of downtown hotels, consider a vacation property in nearby Mount Pleasant.

Napa Valley, North California

Northern California's wine lover's paradise is one of the ideal getaway spots. When couples need a break from being wined and fed, they can join a guided kayak excursion of the picturesque Napa River. Alternatively, get a treatment at one of the area's premier spas.

Virginia

Last but not the least; make a reservation at an oceanfront resort in Virginia Beach, the country's third-largest beach. Alternatively, book a room in a charming inn in Virginia's wine country. The award-winning French restaurant on the site has over 820 wines on offer to enjoy with your lover.

Lastly, remember there's no need for a national holiday dedicated to love to justify spending time with the one you care about. You only need a romantic destination. These couple-friendly getaway spots don't require a passport to visit.
