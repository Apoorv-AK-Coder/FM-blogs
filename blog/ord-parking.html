Overview of O'Hare ORD Parking Options and Rates

Blog       Overview of O'Hare ORD Parking Options and Rates


Planning for the trip through Chicago and not sure about the parking cost at the airport, means you are in the right place. There is a variety of lots customized to different needs, and from quick pick-ups to long-term stays, these rates can vary. This guide helps you to know more about the breakdown of ORD parking rates, and it enables you to choose the most suitable and economical option for your travel.

Parking at the Orlando International Airport offers options based on proximity to terminals and length of stay. Generally, the closer you are to the terminal, the higher the Rate. For the most current pricing, you must check the official website of the Chicago O'Hare Airport, as rates can change.

Main Garage (Lot A) - Hourly and Daily Parking

This option is actually the closest parking option for domestic terminals 1,2, and 3 and making it ideal for short-term stays and passenger meet and greets. This option is perfect for short-term parking, domestic terminals, and convenience.

Rates:

First Hour: $3.00

1 to 2 Hours: $6.00

2 to 3 Hours: $10.00

3 to 4 Hours: $14.00

4 to 8 Hours: $32.00

8 to 24 Hours (Daily Maximum): $42.00

Daily Lots (Lots B & C)

The daily lots of Chicago airport parking are also located just a short walk from the Main Garage, and the Daily Lots offer a slightly more affordable rate for those parking for a full day or more. These lots are ideal for day trips with convenient access to domestic terminals. These lots share the same rate structure as the Main Garage, with a daily maximum of $42.

International Terminal Lot D

This lot is situated adjacent to Terminal 5, serving international travellers. This terminal is ideal for short-term parking for international flights, and the rate structure is the same as the Main Garage, with a daily maximum rate of $42.

Economy Lots (Lots F, G, H) - The Budget-Friendly Choice

For long-term parking, the Economy Lots are the most cost-effective solution. They are connected to all terminals via the free, 24/7 Airport Transit System (ATS). This option is ideal for long-term travel and budget-conscious flyers. For the first Hour, you need to pay $3.00, and for 1 to 2 hours, you need to pay $6.00

Lots G & H Rates:

The daily Rate for Lots G and H is $15.

Daily Rate: $15.00 (Flat rate per 24 hours)

Valet Parking

People who love the utmost convenience can choose valet parking. This parking is best for those travellers who are in a hurry and those with heavy luggage. The ORD parking rates for Valet parking are $34.00 for 0 to 8 hours.

Pre-Booking Online for Potential Savings

This is the international airport, and this airport offers the ability to reserve parking online through its official website. Pre-booking helps you spot that it can often result in lower rates compared to the drive-up prices listed above, especially for the Main Garage and Daily Lots.
