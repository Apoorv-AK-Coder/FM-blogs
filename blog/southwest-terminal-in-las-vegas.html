Southwest Airlines is in Terminal 1 at Las Vegas Airport

Well, for all departures and arrivals, with Southwest Airlines at Harry Reid International Airport, that you will be using is Terminal 1. This terminal is dedicated to the domestic flights of Southwest operations from check-in in bag to boarding your flight.

Key Southwest Locations in Terminal 1:

Check-in and Ticketing: Level 1 of Terminal 1.

Baggage Claim: Level 1 of Terminal 1.

Departure Gates: B and C Gates.

Navigating to Your Southwest Gate

Now, let’s talk about the different gates because once you have checked in and cleared security in terminal 1 and you will proceed to your departure gate. The airline also uses the B and C gates.

B Gates

Once you pass through the A or B security checkpoint, turn left and proceed down the corridor to access the B gates.

C Gates

This gate is accessible via a short automated ram ride from the main area of Terminal 1 and look for signs for the tram to the C gates.

Before going towards any gate, you check your boarding pass and the flight information display screens at the airport for the most up-to-date gate assignment for your particular flight.
