Top Attractions at the U.S. Space & Rocket Center

1. Saturn V Rocket – A National Treasure

The major highlight of the museum is the Saturn V Rocket. This is the National Historic Landmark and only three in the world. This is a powerful rocket that played an important role in NASA's Apollo Mission, including the historic moon landing in 1969.

2. Space Shuttle Pathfinder

You can see a full-scale space shuttle named Pathfinder used for testing in the 1970s. It is a perfect place to take photos and learn about NASA's Space Shuttle Program.

3. Interactive Space Simulators

Experience what it's like to be an astronaut with the G-Force Accelerator and Moonshot Simulators. These rides replicate the forces astronauts experience during space travel, making it a thrilling activity for visitors.

4. INTUITIVE® Planetarium

INTUITIVE® Planetarium space shows that allow visitors to explore the universe, planets in high-definition, and galaxies. It is one of the most advanced planetariums in the country.

5. ISS: Science on Orbit Exhibit

Do you want to explore the International Space Station? This exhibit gives a close-up look at how astronauts eat, sleep, and conduct experiments while living in space. You can even enjoy the real-time life of astronauts here.

How to Get to the U.S. Space & Rocket Center?

The attraction is located at Tranquility Base, Huntsville, AL 35805. You can get there by car, by air, and by train. It is easily accessible via Interstate 565, with on-site parking available. The nearest airport is Huntsville International Airport (HSV), about a 15-minute drive from the center. You can also use the GPS navigation to reach the destination.
