Because of the preferable seats, people need to consider the early bird check-in of Southwest flights, and you will be able to find a good position on the plane because it is the best way to find the available seats.

What is Early Bird Check-In on Southwest Airlines?

Well, this airline does not assign seats in advance. Instead, passengers are assigned a boarding group (A, B, or C) and a position (1–60) based on when they check-in. While selecting the early bird check-in, the airline automatically checks you in 36 hours before departure, which is 12 hours earlier than standard check-in. This helps you to get a better boarding position and more seating choices.

Benefits of Early Bird Check-In

Better Boarding Position – More chances of getting a window, aisle, or front-row seat.

No Need to Rush for Check-In: Southwest handles the check-in process for you.

Sit with Family or Friends – Higher boarding priority increases the chances of sitting together.

More Overhead Bin Space – Board earlier and secure space for your carry-on luggage.

How Much Does Early Bird Check-In Cost?

This is only $15 – $25 per person per flight segment (Price varies based on demand and route).

How to Purchase Early Bird Check-In?

You can buy Early Bird Check-In:

During flight booking on Southwest’s website or app.

After booking (up to 36 hours before departure).

By calling Southwest customer service.

Who Should Get Early Bird Check-In?

Flyers who want a guaranteed good seat. If you want to choose the window seat only then you can choose the early bird check-in.

Families and groups who want to sit together can become part of Southwest Airlines' early bird check-in program.

Passengers who have large luggage with them and want to avoid the last-minute hassles.

You can avail the stress-free check-in experience while using this service by the airline.

Is Early Bird Check-In Worth It?

If you want to choose the best seat for you on the plan, then the early boarding fee at Southwest Airlines is worth it for you because, for the extra convenience, you can invest in better seating options. However, if you don’t mind waiting for a later boarding group, then you can save money and manually check in 24 hours before departure.

Final Thoughts – Should You Get Early Bird Check-In?

Southwest Airlines' early bird check-in is the right option for passengers who want a hassle-free experience and better seat choices. You have to pay only a small fee, and you can avoid the last-minute check-in stress, and you can improve your travel experience.

What is Early Bird Check-In on Southwest Airlines?

It’s a paid service that automatically checks you in 36 hours before departure, giving you a better boarding position.

How much does Early Bird Check-In cost?

It costs $15 – $25 per person, per flight segment, depending on demand and route.

How does Early Bird Check-In help with seating?

It improves your boarding position, increasing your chances of getting a window, aisle, or front-row seat.

No, but it gives you a better position than those who check in manually.

Can I buy Early Bird Check-In after booking my flight?

Yes, you can purchase it at the time of booking or up to 36 hours before departure.

Travelers who want a good seat, sit with family, or need overhead bin space should consider it.

Does Early Bird Check-In apply to all flights?

Yes, it applies to all Southwest Airlines domestic and international flights.

Can I use Early Bird Check-In for a group booking?

Yes, but each passenger must purchase it separately to benefit.

No, once purchased, it is non-refundable.
