Southwest Airlines Checked Bag Policy

You can carry two bags free of charge in the checked baggage. However, there is a size and weight restriction for the people. This is the major perk for the people that they can carry two free checked baggage with this airline and this airline is a budget-friendly option for the flyers.

Weight Limit for Checked Bags

The weight limit for checked luggage on Southwest Airlines Flight is 50 pounds (22.7 kg) per bag. If your bag exceeds this limit, additional fees will apply. Here’s how it breaks down:

Bags Weighing Over 50 Pounds but Under 100 Pounds: Subject to a $75 overweight bag fee.

Bags Weighing Over 100 Pounds: Not accepted as checked luggage and must be shipped via Southwest Cargo.

Size Limit for Checked Bags

On the other hand, while following the weight limit, there are some size limits as well for the checked luggage. The total dimensions of a checked bag (length + width + height) must not exceed 62 inches (157 cm). Oversized bags that exceed these dimensions will also incur a $75 fee.

If your bag is slightly overweight, consider wearing heavier clothing items, like jackets or boots, to free up space and reduce the bag’s weight.
