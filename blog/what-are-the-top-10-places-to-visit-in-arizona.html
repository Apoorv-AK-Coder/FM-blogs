Top 10 Places To Explore In Arizona

Arizona is huge and a proper idea of where to frequent and what to skip is required. Hence the list of all the must-visit places when in Arizona is as follows-

  1. Grand Canyon South Rim- This canyon is the greatest point to visit when in Arizona. This peak holds one of the most beautiful landscape views in Arizona.
  2. Antelope Canyon– This canyon is a beauty in itself. When in Arizona you must tour this place and experience the sheer beauty it beholds.
  3. Horseshoe Bend- This is a great river canyon in Arizona. When in Arizona this river canyon is a must-visit.
  4. Desert Botanical Garden– When in Arizona do not miss touring this unique botanical garden in a desert. This phenomenon might be a first for you to experience but only in Arizona.
  5. Cathedral Rock- This is one of the toughest hikes in Arizona. If you are a pro hiker this is just your spot to hike up.
  6. Chapel Of Holy Cross- The most unique part of this Chapel is that it is formed on top of magnificent rock formations. There is the most beautiful sunset view from this place that one can experience.
  7. Devils Bridge Trail- If you are a thrill lover then this is just your spot to travel. This devil's bridge is not for weak hearts to cross.
  8. Southwest Wildlife Conservation Centre – Arizona is greatly famous for its rich habitat and wildlife. Experience just that at this conservation center.
  9. Musical Instrument Museum- This museum in Arizona is of great significance, especially to people who share an immense love for the art. See the different types of musical instruments from around the world at this place.
  10. Hole In The Rock- If you are a beginner hiker then this is just your place to hike up. The ideal place to hike up for beginners traveling to Arizona.
