Types of Southwest Airlines Visa Cards

The airline offers different types of Visa credit cards right now to cater to the demands of different travelers, from economy class to business class.

Southwest Rapid Rewards® Plus Credit Card

This card is ideal for casual travelers looking for affordable annual fees.

Annual Fee: $69

Key Features:

You can earn 40,000 points after spending $1,000 in the first three months.

2x points on Southwest Airlines purchases and select travel partners.

3,000-anniversary bonus points.

Southwest Rapid Rewards® Premier Credit Card

The card is great for frequent travelers who want enhanced benefits.

Annual Fee: $99

Key Features:

You can earn 60,000 points after spending $3,000 in the first three months.

2x points on Southwest purchases and 1x on all other purchases.

6,000-anniversary bonus points.

No foreign transaction fees.

Southwest Rapid Rewards® Priority Credit Card

The card is mainly designed for travelers seeking premium perks.

Annual Fee: $149

Key Features:

People can earn 75,000 points after spending $5,000 in the first three months.

2x points on Southwest purchases and select travel expenses.

7,500-anniversary bonus points.

$75 Southwest travel credit annually.

Four upgraded boarding passes per year.

The airline offers different types of Visa credit cards, and you can use all these cards for many amazing benefits of travel. Here are the benefits of using Southwest Airlines Visa cards.

2. Companion Pass Qualification

You can also use all these points earned on the Southwest airline credit card toward the coveted Southwest Companion Pass. It helps you to bring a companion on your flights for only taxes and fees.

3. Anniversary Bonus

The Southwest Visa Credit Card also offers yearly bonuses and loyalty rewards to passengers.

4. No Blackout Dates

Yes, this is true that there are no blackout dates on the points that you eared on the Southwest Airlines flight Visa Credit Card.

5. No Expiration of Points

As long as your account remains open, your points won’t expire.

6. Travel Protections

People can enjoy benefits like lost luggage reimbursement, trip delay insurance, and purchase protection.
