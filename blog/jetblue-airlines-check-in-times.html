US Domestic Flights Check-In Time of JetBlue:

If you are traveling in the JetBlue Airlines Flightsfor the US Domestic Flights Route then your bag must be checked at the airport 40 minutes before your flight departure. It is important for the flyers to complete the check-in process of baggage early to avoid the last-minute hassles.

International Flights Check-In Time of JetBlue:

On the other hand, if you are traveling on the International routes then your bag must be checked at the airport 60 minutes before your flight departure timing.
