Gels, Liquids and Aerosols:

There are some rules and restrictions of Transportation Security Administration for the packing gels, aerosols and liquids in the carry-on baggage of the United Airlines. A traveler must use containers to carry these items with the measure of 3.4 ounces or less. These items must be inside a single quart-size or liter-size clear, sealable bag. This rule is not applicable for liquids such as baby formula and medicine and duty-free liquids in the carry-on bags.

Powder Based Substances:

If you are travelling with any item that is in the form of powder then keep the item in your checked bag not in the carry-on bag for the smooth on-boarding. As per the rules of TSA, customers who are travelling to or within the United States not allowed to pack powder based stuff greater than 12 fluid ounces in the carry-on bags and that’s why it must pack in the checked bag. This rule is not applicable for the medically prescribed powders, medicines, baby formula and duty free powders. Passengers can also visit at the portal of DHS to know more about the rules.

Musical instruments As Carry-On Bags:

A passenger can easily carry the musical instruments as the carry-on bag such as violin, guitar or other small stuff in the hard case. These items will be counted as the carry-on bag and must fit in the overhead bin space of the United Airlines.
