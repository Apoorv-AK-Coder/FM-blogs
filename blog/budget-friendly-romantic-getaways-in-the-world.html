1. Santorini, Greece: A Mediterranean Vacation for Couples on a Budget

Sail along those Sinquinese iconic sunsets, their brilliant white architecture, and sparkling sapphire-blue waters, which spell romance. And although it is best known as the place where the elite have their holiday, it doesn’t mean you cannot travel there on a budget.

  • Try the quieter guesthouses in Fira or Perissa instead of splurging on expensive hotels in Oia. Enjoy dining in family-run tavernas with authentic Greek dishes like moussaka and souvlaki at budget prices.
  • Take advantage of free opportunities like hiking the Fira-to-Oia trail, which gives breathtaking scenery, interesting experiences, and shooting views. Travelling off-season will give you the attraction with fewer crowds at cheaper prices. Use public buses. It is the cheapest way to travel between the towns and beaches.
  • Catch a sunset moment at Akrotiri Lighthouse, which is less crowded than others, or have a quiet day at Kamari Beach.

2. Ubud, Bali: Tranquillity in Tropical Paradise

Tucked in the very heart of Bali is Ubud, a couple’s hideaway when it comes to peace and romance. With its rice terraces and temples, tranquil and yet inexpensive luxury, Ubud is just perfect as a budget getaway. Enjoy cosy and pretty villas and boutique guest houses that offer comfort without high costs.

  • The wonderful sights of the Tegallalang Rice Terraces or Campuhan Ridge Walk can be used as backdrops to produce pictures of romantic couples.
  • With spa treatments for couples priced at a fraction of that charged in other venues, this is an experience for couples.
  • Eat-in warungs for delicious Balinese food at rock-bottom prices where delectable dishes are served in very warm atmospheres.
  • Discover the art scene of Ubud or enjoy a performance of traditional Kecak dance to taste culture.

3. Prague, Czech Republic: A Fairy-Tale Romance on a Budget

Prague’s ageless charm, with its cobbled streets and historic architecture, gives romanticism a magical setting. Dubbed the “City of a Hundred Spires,” it is affordable for couples on a Eurotrip honeymooning together.

  • Book budget rooms around Old Town to eat hearty Czech meals in traditional pubs. Stroll hand-in-hand across Charles Bridge or climb Petrin Hill for breathtaking views of the city.
  • Visit Prague Castle and discover the multi-coloured Lennon Wall, as well as roaming in the beautiful streets of MalaStrana—all without smashing a piggy bank.

  • Be it watching the chime of the Astronomical Clock or listening to the love songs by the Vltava River sunsets, Prague is the real winner for romance on a budget.

4. Tulum, Mexico: Boho-Chic Love by the Sea

The beautiful beaches, cerulean waters, and a lovely bohemian aura of Tulum make it a romantic getaway, but fortunately, it is one of those destinations where the couple can afford the log hut.

  • Beautiful places of accommodation are eco-friendly cabanas to budget boutique hostels within the jungle.
  • Enjoy true tacos and fresh ceviche without scrimping on taste and find out where it’s served on the street from stalls.
  • A day visiting these Mayan ruins facing the sea or snorkelling in clear cenotes like Dos Ojos or Gran Cenote is a great way to spend the day.
  • Enjoy some time at the public beaches in Tulum so you can soak in the amazing ocean scenery without having to pay for it at all. Rent bikes to navigate the area at a low cost and in an eco-friendly fashion.

5. Budapest, Hungary: Become a Spa City for Romantic

Budapest—the “Spa Capital of the World”—is” a striking feature, balancing history and relaxation with romance.

  • It is a prime city for couples where you can visit a few of the famous thermal baths—Szechenyi and Gellert spas—where you can have a few moments of soaking in the healing waters at a very reasonable price.
  • Walk along the Danube River and take in the view of the Parliament Building and Buda Castle. The budget-friendly sunset river cruise is a great thing to do together.
  • Explore the historic streets of the Jewish Quarter, and indulge in some hearty traditional Hungarian food at little local bistros where you won’t break the bank.
  • Budget-friendly accommodation options near the centre are available, maximising walking or public transport access to all sites. From having a luxurious soak in the spa to a leisurely walk at the river, romance fills every corner of Budapest.

6. A place that blends adventure and magic romance: Cape Town, South Africa

Adventure and romance come packaged together on a budget in the city of Cape Town.

  • Couples can hike Table Mountain for a panoramic view of the city and the entire coastline or take a scenic drive through the Cape Peninsula to enjoy the most beautiful beaches and fishing villages.
  • Adrenaline junkies may take a day trip to the famous Cape of Good Hope, or they may go spot the little penguins at Boulders Beach, and most of the activities tend to be cheap or free.
  • The affordable guesthouses and boutique hotels provide cosy yet charming stays in the city.
  • Enjoy the very busy V&A Waterfront; join some time together with yourself and nature at the Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens; or opt for a wine-tasting tour in the nearby Stellenbosch region.

7. Serenity in God’s Own Country: Kerala

God’s own country, Kerala, brings forth a romantic vacation on a little budget.

  • For example, a houseboat ride in Alleppey or Kumarakom across the backwaters is full of scenic views and soothing waters promising peace and intimacy. The romance will escalate by visiting the tea gardens of Munnar or taking a quiet walk through the spice plantations in Thekkady.
  • Beaches like Varkala and Kovalam are built for sunset strolls and lazy days watching over the sea. Ayurvedic treatments are popular and cheap in this region and offer rejuvenation for couples.
  • Stay at homestays or budget resorts to soak in the natural charm of Kerala. It makes it extra convenient for you to find flights that are beyond affordable, and you’ll include every bit of Kerala in your pocket.
  • For this reason, Kerala is perfectly famous for a couple seeking an alluring getaway, rich in culture and integrated with nature for couples seeking peaceful romantic space.
