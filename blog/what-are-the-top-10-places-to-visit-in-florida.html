Top 10 Must-See Spots in Florida:

Kennedy Space Center: Discover the wonders of space and learn about NASA's incredible missions.

Walt Disney World: Experience the magic of Disney with exciting rides and attractions for all ages.

Universal Studios: Enjoy thrilling rides and shows based on popular movies.

Miami Beach and the Art Deco District: Relax on beautiful beaches and admire the unique architecture.

Everglades National Park: Explore the unique wetlands filled with wildlife, including alligators and exotic birds.

Daytona 500 International Speedway: Experience the thrill of NASCAR at this famous racetrack.

SeaWorld Orlando: Get up close with marine life through exciting shows and attractions.

Busch Gardens Tampa: Enjoy roller coasters and see amazing animals at this African-themed park.

Dry Tortugas National Park: Visit this beautiful park known for its coral reefs and historic Fort Jefferson.

Duval Street in Key West: Walk down this lively street filled with shops, bars, and restaurants.
