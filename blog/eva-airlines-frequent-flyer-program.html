What Is Infinity MileageLands?

This is the airline's frequent flyer program, specially designed to reward passengers with miles who fly with Eva and its partner airlines. These miles can be used for different purchases such as flight tickets, upgrades, extra baggage allowance, and exclusive member privileges.

How to Join Infinity MileageLands?

Joining the program is a relatively easy task for the flyers because this is very simple, and there is no joining fee for this program. You can simply enroll in the Infinity MileageLands.

  1. Go to the official website of Eva Airlines, or you can also download the Eva Airlines app.
  2. No will the member form of Infinity MileageLands.
  3. Add your details and cross-check the details twice to avoid any errors.
  4. Now, you will receive the membership number on your email or phone number.
  5. By using the membership number, you can check the miles.

Earning Miles in Eva Flights

It is very easy for flyers with Infinity MileageLands because they can flexibly earn miles by flying with Eva Air. Yes, you can earn miles every time when you fly with this airline. Earn miles depends on the fare type, ticket class and distance traveled. People who travel in business and premium economy class usually get a higher mile rate than economy class fares.

What are the partner airlines?

Eva is a member of the Star Alliance, and that’s why allowing members to earn miles on flights operated by partners such as Lufthansa, Singapore, and United Airlines is quite easy for the flyers. That means if you book the tickets for United, Lufthansa and Singapore, then you can also earn miles because you are a member of Eva's frequent flyer program.

What about hotel and car rental partners?

Well, the Infinity MileageLands program also allows the partner with several hotel and car rental services, and members can earn miles on the rentals as well. Not only this, but people can also earn miles on co-branded credit cards, and by using the Eva Air co-branded card, you can earn miles on everyday purchases. On the other hand, redeeming miles is also easy from this program. And you can redeem miles on these things -

Award Flights: Book tickets with EVA Air or partner airlines using your accumulated miles.

Seat Upgrades: Upgrade to Business Class or Premium Economy for a more luxurious travel experience.

Extra Baggage Allowance: Use miles to cover excess baggage fees.

Exclusive Rewards: Redeem miles for gifts, vouchers, or services from program partners.

Membership Tiers and Benefits

There are around four membership ties of the Infinity MileageLands, Eva frequent flyer program, and these are

  1. Entry Level Green Card. This is the basic earning and redemption privileges-based tier of the airline program. Through this program, people can also access member-exclusive promotions.
  2. The second tier is the Silver Card, which helps passengers get priority check-in and boarding services. Flyers can earn a 10% mileage bonus on Eva Air-operated flights.
  3. Third-one is the Gold Card, and if you have this tier of Infinity MileageLands, then you can access Eva Air VIP lounges and avail all the exclusive services that you want. You will get the benefit of extra baggage allowance along with a 15% mileage bonus. The fourth tier is the Dimond Card, and this is among the top-tier benefits with all priority services, complimentary upgrades and a 25% mileage bonus.

Star Alliance Benefits

Eva is a member of Star Alliance, and that’s why Infinity MileageLands also comes with many more global advances, such as

Earning and redeeming miles on over 25 Star Alliance airlines.

Access to over 1,000 lounges worldwide for eligible tier members.

Seamless travel across the network with priority services.

FAQs

What is EVA Air's Infinity MileageLands program?

Infinity MileageLands is EVA Air's frequent flyer program that allows passengers to earn and redeem miles on flights, upgrades, and partner services.

How does Star Alliance membership enhance Infinity MileageLands?

Star Alliance membership allows members to earn and redeem miles on over 25 global partner airlines, providing greater flexibility and access to worldwide travel benefits.

What are the benefits of the Infinity MileageLands Green Card?

The Green Card offers basic earning and redemption privileges, access to member-exclusive promotions, and serves as the entry point to EVA Air’s loyalty benefits.

How does Infinity MileageLands differ from traditional loyalty programs?

Infinity MileageLands integrates with a broader partner network, including hotels, car rentals, and co-branded credit cards, offering diverse ways to earn and redeem miles.

What is the easiest way to join Infinity MileageLands?

Enrollment is free and can be completed online via the EVA Air website or mobile app, making it accessible and straightforward.

What makes EVA Air's Diamond Card tier unique?

The Diamond Card provides top-tier benefits like complimentary upgrades, priority services, a 25% mileage bonus, and access to exclusive VIP lounges.

How can members earn miles beyond flying with EVA Air?

Members can earn miles through partner airlines, hotel stays, car rentals, and purchases made using co-branded credit cards.

What is the cheapest way to redeem miles for travel?

Redeeming miles for seat upgrades or award flights during off-peak seasons provides the best value for members.

How do EVA Air’s frequent flyer benefits compare to its competitors?

EVA Air offers comprehensive benefits like higher earning rates in premium classes and a more inclusive partner network, making it more versatile than many competitors.

Why should occasional travelers consider joining Infinity MileageLands?

Even occasional travelers can benefit from miles that never expire (with account activity) and redeem them for upgrades or extra baggage, making it valuable for infrequent flyers.
