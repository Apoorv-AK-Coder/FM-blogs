Below is the list of some safest destinations for solo travellers.

Iceland

The crime rate in Iceland is meagre, and the country has some of the best healthcare systems in the world. Iceland has been a safe country to visit for many years. The country has a low risk of life.

With added reassurance, Iceland is one of the most popular destinations for solo backpackers. You can go whale watching in Reykjavik. Other ocean-going tours like bird watching and island visits are also available.

The Northern Lights or the Aurora Borealis is one of the most popular tourist attractions worldwide. The spectacular natural light show is best admired in remote places. You can also hike in Landmannalaugar Nature Reserve, which features multi-hued rhyolite mountains, Hekla volcano, and extensive lava fields. The place is best for horseback riding and hiking.

New Zealand

On the GPI list of safest countries, New Zealand ranks second. Each year nearly 200,000 British travel to New Zealand, and most of them face no trouble. New Zealand is the world's most peaceful and least corrupt country and is a good choice for solo backpackers. Many exciting tourist attractions are there in New Zealand.

Explore the Bay of Islands, a haven for yacht and sailing boats. You can go for a hike on the island trails and explore the undistributed forest. Stroll around Rotorua and experience the geothermal wonders. Soak yourself in the mineral-rich water. Adventures like skydiving, lugging, trekking and mountain biking make New Zealand the best option for tourists with an adventurous soul.

Portugal

Portugal has a low crime rate. Portugal ranks third among 163 countries when it comes to overall safety. Therefore the country is a safe place for solo travel. The cosmopolitan cities, towns and villages, lovely beach resorts and national parks make Portugal one of the best tourist attractions. Visit Lisbon, which is one of Europe's most captivating cities.

Here you will find open plazas, beautiful cathedrals and small cafes where you can spend some time. The beaches in Algarve are surely going to blow your mind. The destination offers some of the best hiking activities. International surfing events like windsurfing and water skiing take place yearly. If you are an animal lover, you must visit Peneda Here's, National Park.

Austria

Austria is one of the safest destinations. Compared to other countries, the robbery and assault rates are the lowest. Violent crimes rarely occur in this country. The country ranks fourth on the GPI list of safest countries.

You can soak yourself in the old-world charm of Old Town and take a trip to the Salzburg Museum. Take a stroll in the Mirabell Gardens and tour the famous residence of Mozart.

With an extensive range of mountains, skiing is one of the most adventurous activities most solo travellers enjoy. You can also ride on the cable car to the summits of Kitzbüheler Horn and enjoy the beautiful view from a height of 2000 metres. Adventurous visitors must go hiking, bungee jumping at the Europa Bridge, and check out the Innsbruck nightlife.

Denmark

Denmark is an overall safe country to visit for solo travellers. The visitors have little to worry about in terms of crime and theft. Denmark is one of the happiest countries in the world. Visit Rabjerg Mile, one of the top migrating dunes and one of the best places you visit in Denmark.

You can explore the dune on a bike. Try to visit the famous fishing town, Gilleleje and experience the cool breeze. You can see fishers clearing the lines besides the scenic fishing town.

If you want to get a sneak peek at the history of Denmark, then Elsinore is a must-place to visit. The city has several house libraries, exhibition halls, and a shipyard museum. The Tivoli Garden amusement park is the most proper place to visit for a beautiful experience.

Canada

Canada ranks sixth in the list of GPI and is undoubtedly one of the safest places to visit as a solo traveller. The crime rates in the country are low, and the police are trusted, easy to contact, and they respond quickly.

You will have a great time enjoying the views and clicking pictures of Niagara Falls. Spend some quality time at Marine land. In winter, Canada is the best place for skiing, snowshoeing, and tobogganing. These thrilling sports will make your vacation even more memorable.

You can also visit various art museums and cultural centres to witness the country's vibrant culture. Try visiting the Cape Spear Lighthouse and learn about the local culture and history at The Rooms. It would be best if you also explored famous beaches like Long Beach and Chester man Beach. Indulge yourself in kayaking and camping at the Pacific Rim National Park Reserve.

Singapore

Crime rates in Singapore are meagre compared to other destinations because the country is heavily policed. Strong punishments are imposed on those who break the laws and attempt crimes. Singapore is a favourite spot for food lovers where they will enjoy diverse and innovative cuisines. The cuisines are of a high standard and affordable budget.

Solo backpackers will never feel lonely in this country. Take a stroll through the Bay East Garden to enjoy the vibrant plant life and escape the city bustle for a moment. You can head towards the Cloud Forest Dome to see the world's tallest indoor waterfall. Visit the Singapore zoo to encounter various species. The Orchard Road area is a great place for shopaholics. Try visiting Chinatown during your stay.
