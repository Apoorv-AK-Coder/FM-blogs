Classes of Travel in Scandinavian Airlines

When travelling on SAS flights you are introduced to three different classes of seating. This airline allows you to choose the class of seating that will suit you the very best when you are travelling. The different classes of seatings presented are as follows

SAS Go Smart: This class can be equalised to the economy class of other flights. Choosing this seat will allow you baggage benefits and the choice of choosing your seat when checking in.

SAS Plus Smart: This is a well-upgraded class of seating for travellers. When choosing this seat one gains the ability to choose their seats at checking in, baggage benefits along with SAS lounge access and fast track through the security line as well.

SAS Business Smart: This class of seating is equivalent to the business class seating which will be available on an airline. Flying in this class will give you the benefit of a fully flat bed, premium quality of service lounge access baggage benefits also fast track through security.

SAS Check-In

You can check in much more easily when flying this airline. SAS Airlines Check-In can be done through main two methods. Both these methods are very easy to carry out. You can alternate them at your convenience. The two methods which are shown are elaborated as the following

Online Check-in: You can follow this process of checking in if you want to avoid long queues at the airport. You can easily process your check in from thirty hours to one hour before the flight departure. This process can be conducted with much more easily through the official website of the Scandinavian Airlines. After this you will have to print out your boarding pass and your luggage tags.

Airport Check-in: This is a more traditional way of check-in indeed. The catch though is the existence of kiosks nowadays at airports, you can process your check-in at the kiosks present at the airport and skip queues at your convenience. The check-in at the airport usually always opens two hours before your flight departure.
