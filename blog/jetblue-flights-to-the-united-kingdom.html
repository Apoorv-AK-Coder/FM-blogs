Popular Places to Visit in the U.K.

There are numerous attractions in the U.K. that date back to the pre-historic, ancient, medieval, and modern era. It is one of the oldest countries in the world and has many historic attractions. The cultural heritage and magnificent monuments of these cities will keep you spellbound. Each of them has something extraordinary that you wouldn't miss. The popular must-visit cities in the U.K. are:

  1. London is the largest metropolis in the U.K. The Tower of London and Buckingham Palace are the must-see attractions.
  2. Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland. The ancient tradition blends with the modern vitality, alluring the beauty of the city.
  3. Oxford is a remarkable city and home to one of the oldest universities. Its rich history and culture immerse the visitors.
  4. Cambridge is famous for the scenic Cambridge University and the unique history of the city.
  5. Bath is a UNESCO World Heritage Site with its elegant Georgian architecture.
  6. York is a medieval gem situated by the Great Ouse River. It is a magnificent city holding its importance in British history.
  7. Manchester is a city that is rich in history. It's a famous city that contributed to various fields such as music, football, the Industrial Revolution, etc.
  8. Liverpool is a UNESCO World Heritage site built on maritime heritage.
  9. Birmingham is a multicultural and dynamic city with people from diverse and vibrant communities.
  10. Glasgow is a city situated on the banks of River Clyde and is famous for its shipbuilding heritage.
