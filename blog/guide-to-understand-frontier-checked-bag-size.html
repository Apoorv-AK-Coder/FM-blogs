Checked Bag Size & Weight Limits

Maximum dimensions: 62 linear inches (length + width + depth), including handles, wheels, and straps

Maximum weight: 40 lb (18.1 kg) or less

Bags exceeding either limit may incur fees or be refused

What is the additional fee for an oversized bag on Frontier Airlines?

Additional Fees

Overweight (41–50 lb): + $75 per bag, per direction

Overweight (51–100 lb): + $100 per bag, per direction

Bags over 100 lb: Not accepted

Oversize (63–110 linear inches): + $75 per bag, per direction

Bags over 110 inches: Not accepted

Exceptions: Special Items & Military

There are some items in the baggage policy for which airlines offer exceptions, and the essential devices are medical devices, baby gear, strollers, wheelchairs. These devices are allowed without counting toward your baggage allowance and are not subject to standard size/weight limits or fees. The Active U.S. military personnel may check up to two bags for free and are expect from overweight or oversize charges for their first two checked items.

Real-World Notes & Enforcement

The airline gate agents measure bags at the gate and may require fitting into their sizer and even for personal items or bag sizes that meet published limits.
