Checked Baggage Allowance and Fees

The airline does not include the checked baggage, and it is a standard fare, and all checked bags incur additional fees.

Size and Weight Limits:

The size and weight limit is applicable for the baggage fee of Frontier Airlines flights, and you need to follow the dimensions rules of the airline. You can also read the baggage policy of the airline on the website of the airline. Sometimes, baggage fees vary or are updated by the airline, and all the updated information is available on the website. Sixty-two linear inches are the maximum dimensions for the checked bag (length + width + height). The maximum weight limit of a checked bag is 40 pounds. If your bag weight limit is more than the given dimensions and weight, then you have to pay the checked baggage fee of Frontier Airlines.

Oversized Bags:

The bags measuring between 63 and 110 linear inches incur a $75 fee per bag per direction.

The bag size exceeding 110 linear inches is not accepted.

Bags exceeding 110 linear inches are not accepted.

Overweight Bags:

41 to 50 pounds: $75 per bag, per direction.

51 to 100 pounds: $100 per bag, per direction.

Bags over 100 pounds are not accepted.

2. You need to use the bundles because the airline also offers bundles like “The Works” and “The Perks,” and this includes a carry-on and checked bag, potentially saving you money.
