FAQs

What is the best time to visit Costa Rica?

The best time to visit Costa Rica is from December to April, as this is the dry season when you can explore the country’s beauty to the fullest. November is ideal for exploring national parks, and the green season runs from May to June for those who enjoy lush landscapes and rainfall.

What is the cheapest time to visit Costa Rica?

The cheapest time to visit Costa Rica is during the low season, typically March when airfare and hotel prices are more affordable. This time is perfect for travellers looking to save money while enjoying the destination with fewer crowds.

Why is December to April considered the best time to visit Costa Rica?

December to April is the dry season, offering pleasant weather ideal for outdoor activities, and exploring the country's natural attractions. This period has less rainfall, making it easier to travel and enjoy Costa Rica’s diverse landscape and wildlife.

Why is March the cheapest time to fly to Costa Rica from the USA?

March falls under the low season for tourism in Costa Rica, meaning fewer crowds and lower demand for flights and hotels. This results in more affordable airfare and travel packages for visitors.

Which airlines offer flights to Costa Rica from the USA?

Major airlines offering flights from the USA to Costa Rica include Delta, American Airlines, United Airlines, and Avianca Airlines. However, JetBlue Airlines is the most affordable option for budget-conscious travellers.

Why should travellers choose the cheapest time to visit Costa Rica?

Travelling during the cheapest time allows visitors to save on airfare, hotels, and other travel expenses. The lower crowds during this period make for a more relaxed and cost-effective travel experience.

What are the key attractions in Costa Rica during the rainy season (May)?

May marks the rainy season, offering travellers the chance to experience lush greenery, vibrant nature, and a more tranquil environment. It’s an excellent time for those who love natural landscapes and quieter tourist spots.

What are the starting fares for flights from the USA to Costa Rica?

Airfare for flights from the USA to Costa Rica can start as low as $64, especially when booked with budget-friendly airlines like JetBlue during the low season.
