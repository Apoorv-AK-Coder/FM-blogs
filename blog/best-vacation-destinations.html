The Top 10 Best Seaside Destinations

The best vacation that you would experience in your life is undoubtedly the vacation spent at a location of the sea. Some of the top seaside vacation destinations for all of you to explore are as the following –

  1. Goa, India- This is one of the most beautiful seaside destinations and also undoubtedly one of the most famous ones. The best thing about this place other than its stunning natural beauty is that it is very foreigner-friendly. Leave all your worries and have fun in Goa, partying, swimming, and exploring the natural beauty of the place will have you largely occupied.
  2. Miami, USA- Miami is again a very famous and well-loved seaside destination worldwide. Everyone has heard of the Miami beach at least once in their lives, there is a lot of fascination that is connected to this place. The Cuban culture has a very great influence here.
  3. Hawaii, USA- If there is any beach destination in the USA that has gotten even more popularity than Miami, it would be Hawaii. Not only in the US but around the world Hawaii is known for its beautiful culture and stunning beaches. Hawaii is one of the most significant islands that you require to explore.
  4. Jeju-do, South Korea- Jeju-do island is one of the most prominent and beautiful islands in South Korea that garners much tourist attention from around the world. This island is one of the most beautiful islands in South Korea which has great buzz due to its cultural significance in the country truly.
  5. Aruba, Kingdom Of Netherlands – Aruba is a beautiful island country that comes under the governance of the Kingdom Of Netherlands. This is an island trail with the Caribbean islands, and in one word Aruba is just stunning. The incredibly white sandy beach and turquoise water are magnificent to view and experience.
  6. Bali, Indonesia – Bali is again a very famous island in Indonesia which is much loved by foreigners from all over the world. Not only the seaside but also the surroundings which cover the land is just so stunning. This is a very affordable tourist destination which has gained great fame and influx of tourists in recent years.
  7. Maldives- This is another very famous island country that has been on every girl's travel list forever. Maldives is one of the most famous and takes about islands to visit. A Maldives honeymoon or a Maldives trip with your friends is one of the most precious gifts you can give yourself.
  8. Mauritius – This island country if not more but has enjoyed just as much popularity and love as the perfect destination as Maldives. People love Mauritius not only for its sea sides but also for the beautiful lagoons and the culture that is just so fun to experience.
  9. Sri Lanka – This is a beautiful island country in Asia, and most of its economy is dependent on tourism. This is a more affordable vacation choice but you won't be missing out on a thing when here. The beauty of beaches, the thrill of watersports, and a rich culture await you in this country.
  10. Phuket, Thailand – This is again a well-celebrated and loved island in Bangkok for its beauty. Not only the beach but also the architecture of the city is enough to brighten your mood. You can try many unique delicacies when here.

Things To Carry On Your Beach Vacation

Some things that you must carry when you are on your ideal beach vacation are-

  1. Take enough sunscreen for all, as getting sunburns by the beach is common.
  2. Carry some mat to place on the beach if you want to sit without sand getting all over you.
  3. Take water-appropriate clothes when you are at the beach.
  4. Get some fun beachside games, like a ball for volleyball or tools to build a sand castle to make the most out of your trip.
