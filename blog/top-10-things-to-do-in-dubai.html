Top 10 Things to Do in Dubai: Luxury and Adventure

Dubai is a city of luxury that has always combined adventure and excitement for every traveller. Travelling and discovering the world is not about spending on lavishness, but Dubai has opulence and, of course, thrills. The mighty skyscrapers touch the sky, travelling over dunes and offering you sights that inspire ambition and creativity in every single aspect. 1. Burj Khalifa: Reach for the Sky

  • Everybody who visits Dubai should see the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, with its 828 meters. A true marvel of architecture, it enables one to see things from a whole different view within the city, the desert, and outside of it.
  • It has the "At the Top" on the 148th floor, from where you can see the entire city from a 360-degree viewpoint. On a very clear day, you can see up to 95 kilometres from it, making it one of the most photographed points in the world.
  • For a truly decadent experience, however, try dining at Atmosphere, the world's highest restaurant, located on the 122nd floor. Refined ambience and a gourmet menu make this experience stand above the rest when considering the Burj Khalifa visit.

2. Palm Jumeirah: The Eighth Wonder of the Earth

  • Nothing can be compared to the experience of skydiving above the Palm Jumeirah. This will allow you to free fall from 13,000 feet with the island's palm shape below, and the distance stretches out to the Arabian Gulf.
  • The views make the thrill of skydiving interesting. It gives a unique, rare view from above of one of the most famous man-made creations in the world.
  • Whether you have been skydiving for years or just feel like trying it for the first time, tandem jumps with certified instructors are safe and give you a live-wired moment. On the descent, you'll catch open views of Dubai's skyline, desert, and ocean, making it a once-in-a-lifetime action.

3. Dubai Desert Safari: For Thrill Seekers

  • This desert around Dubai is an adventure lover's paradise, weaving adrenaline and serenity into one. A desert safari starts with that thrilling dune bashing session.
  • Climbing to the top and down the sides of formidable dunes with a 4Ã—4, the ride will just thrill you with the bumps and turns. When you're done with the excitement, settle in for a relaxing camel ride across the golden sands, which will bring you a peaceful view of its beauty.
  • You will also get to enjoy the sunset, as the desert turns out to be a magical scene, and then witness traditional Arabic entertainment such as belly dancing and fire shows. This evening's event will be perfected with an Arabic dinner served under the stars in a traditional Bedouin-type camp.

4. Dubai Mall: Shoppers' Paradise

  • The Dubai Mall is more than just a colossal shopping centre; it is indeed a place where the activities go far beyond shopping; it's an entertainment hub par excellence. It houses more than 1,200 stores, from the finest luxury retailers to international ones; in fact, it is one of the largest malls on earth.
  • After indulging in all the shopping therapy, go check out some of the mall's attractions including the superb Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo, featuring thousands of aquatic life forms, including sharks and rays. For skiing in winter, there is the Dubai Ice Rink, which is a full-sized Olympic skating rink, open for public skating and taking lessons.
  • For families, there is the indoor theme park, the VR Park, as well as the facility to catch the latest blockbuster movies at the cinema located in the mall.

5. Dubai Marina: A Waterfront Retreat

  • Dubai Marina is indeed the perfect getaway for a private yacht cruise, with its beautiful skyline of glittering skyscrapers and luxurious yachts. Rent a yacht to escape into the calm waters of the Arabian Gulf, all while taking in breathtaking views of monuments like the Palm Jumeirah, Burj Al Arab, and the whole Dubai coast.
  • Make your special occasion even more meaningful, gather people into your private celebration, or just while away time in sheer luxury enjoying Dubai's beauty. This experience could not get any more relaxing and luxurious.
  • You can also add gourmet catering and personalised services to your cruise, even entertainment onboard, turning this into a unique experience.

6. Dubai Opera: A Cultural Extravaganza

  • The Dubai Opera is an important cultural destination for the arts and culture lovers. It is a magnificent creation in Dubai and is home to a large variety of performances, namely classical opera, ballet, rock, and many theatre performances.
  • The venue is designed to look like the traditional dhow boat and is intended to offer excellent acoustics.
  • Located in downtown Dubai, this makes for a good evening outing after checking sites, such as Burj Khalifa or Dubai Fountain. Its lively atmosphere cannot be missed among its grandeur.

7. Global Village: A World of Cultures

  • Here, you can observe quite several people from various communities around the world. Some come for a taste of the experience they give out through their food, some through their arts, and some through their entertainment.
  • It is also a seasonal park that has pavilions of countries, each with an experience of its own. It is a place where you can shop for authentic crafts of the world, very many tastes from all around the world, and exciting amusement rides.
  • For family and everybody who likes tasting the world while they don't have to go beyond Dubai, it is the right place to hang out. It is a culture blended with fun and trade.

8. Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood: A Step Back in Time

  • This is a part of the city that really has some glimpse of history showing a bygone era with its traditional wind-tower houses, narrow alleys, and art galleries, which seem to be some oasis from the modernity around.

  • Also, it is that place where you will find some very good cafes and restaurants for enjoying rich, authentic Arabic food.

9. La Mer: A Waterfront Refuge

  • Here in the La Mer, a little sun, a lot of sea and heaps of style meet. La Mer has pristine beaches, chic cafes, and distinctive street art along the Jumeirah coastline.
  • Visitors can simply sunbathe on the soft sand, indulge in water sports, or even wander around shopping and dining options.
  • It also boasts thrilling rides and a lazy river for a family day out having lots of fun. La Mer, with its easygoing vibe and modern facilities, is certainly the place to be after a day's excursion around the many Dubai attractions.

10. Ski Dubai: Winter in the Desert

  • With Ski Dubai, visitors will know how to appreciate snow while in the middle of the desert. Nestled inside the Mall of the Emirates, this indoor ski resort has real snow, thus allowing visitors to ski or snowboard or even play with penguins.
  • It is a mind-blowing experience to be under the snowfall with all that desert heat blazing outside. The park also has a snow park that features chairlifts and sliding hills on their snow.
  • Whether you're an expert skier or a newcomer to the skiing world, Ski Dubai promises to amuse everyone.

Not forgetting the cultural wonders, add in a simply unique experience and the world, and you have something for everybody.
