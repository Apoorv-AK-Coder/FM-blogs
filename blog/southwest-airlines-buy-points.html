How Does Buying Southwest Airlines Points Work?

The Southwest Airlines Buy Points feature allows Rapid Rewards members to purchase additional points when they don’t have enough for flight redemption. These points can be used to book flights, cover taxes and fees, or even earn Companion Pass eligibility.

  • Available for Rapid Rewards members
  • Points can be purchased in increments
  • No blackout dates for redeeming points
  • It is ideal for topping up your balance for a reward flight

Steps to Buy Southwest Airlines Points

  1. Log in to your Southwest Rapid Rewards account.
  2. Go to the “Buy Points” section under the Rapid Rewards menu.
  3. elect the number of points you want to buy.
  4. Complete the payment using a credit or debit card.
  5. Points are credited to your account immediately or within 72 hours.
