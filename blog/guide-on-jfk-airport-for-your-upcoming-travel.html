This is the primary international airport located in Queens, New York City. The airport is located in Queens, New York City, United States. The IATA Code is JFK, and the ICAO Code is KJFK. The airport was opened on July 1, 1948, and was initially known as Idlewild Airport; renamed JFK Airport in 1963 after President John F. Kennedy’s assassination).

Operated by: Port Authority of New York and New Jersey. There are around 62 million passengers (pre-pandemic, 2019), making it one of the most traffic-based airports in North America.

Facts to Know About John F. Kennedy International Airport

  1. This airport is the primary international gateway and is essential for international travel to and from the United States.
  2. The demerit of this airport is known for extensive delays due to high traffic volume, so travellers are advised to allow plenty of time for check-in and security.
  3. JFK Airport is directly connected to Manhattan and other boroughs via the AirTrain System, subways, buses, rideshare services, and taxis.
  4. Terminal layout can be complicated. Therefore, travellers should verify their departure terminal ahead of arrival to avoid confusion.
  5. Also, make sure to arrive early at the airport because the security and customs process at JFK can take more time and extensive. International travellers should ensure all required documentation is prepared in advance.
  6. The official website of JFK Airport is jfkairport.com. You can visit this website to learn about the JFK parking charges and departure and arrival details.

On Airport Parking

Short Term Terminal Garages (Blue, Yellow, Orange, Red):

First 30 minutes:

Blue/Yellow (Terminals 4 & 5): ~$6 per half hour

Orange/Red (Terminals 7–8, 1/2): ~$4 per half hour

After 3 hours:

Blue/Yellow: ~$12 per half hour, capped typically at ~$70/day drive up or ~$42/day prepaid

Orange/Red: ~$8 per half hour after 3 hours; ~$60–65/day drive up, ~$36/day prepaid airportparkingreservations.com

Long Term Economy Lot (Lot 9) & Federal Circle:

Drive up: ~$29/day

Prepaid (book online at least 24 hrs ahead): ~$20/day

Federal Circle Lot (prepaid only): ~$20/day for first 24 hrs, ~$10 per 12 hrs after
