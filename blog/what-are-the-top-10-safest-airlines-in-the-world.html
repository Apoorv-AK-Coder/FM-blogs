Top 10 Safest Airlines in the World

Multiple airlines are known for providing special services such as complimentary refreshments and beverages, last-minute flights and extra baggage facilities for additional prices. In all activities undertaken by airlines, the security and satisfaction of their customers should come first.

Some of the safest airlines in the world are listed here:

  1. Air New Zealand – Air New Zealand is the New Zealander flag carrier and has served the Kiwis for over eighty years since the airline was founded on 26th April 1940 as the Tasman Empire Airways Limited and commenced operations on 1st April 1965 under its current name.
  2. Qantas – Qantas, the Australian flag carrier and one of the largest airlines of its continent – Oceania when ranked in terms of fleet size, international destinations and international flights, has served the country since it was founded on 16th November 1920 and commenced operations on 2nd November two years later.
  3. Virgin Australia – Virgin Australia is one of the most important and well-known airlines of the country Australia and is made up of a unique combination of low-cost airline and standard airline features, attracting many travellers since it was founded on 29th August 2000 and commenced operations on 4th May 2011 under its current name.
  4. Etihad Airways – Etihad Airways is one of the flag carriers of the United Arab Emirates and has often been ranked as the second largest airline in its country since it was founded twenty-one years ago in July of the year 2003 and it commenced operations twenty years ago in November of the same year.
  5. Qatar Airways – Qatar Airways is the Qatari flag carrier and one of the best-known airlines of its continent. It has been repeatedly ranked as one of Asia’s most secure and luxurious airlines since it was founded on 22nd November in the year 1993 and commenced operations on 20th January of the next year.
  6. Emirates – Emirates is one of the United Arab Emirates flag carriers and is considered one of the largest airlines in its area – the Middle East since it was founded thirty-nine years ago on 25th March in the year 1985 and commenced operations on 25th October of the same year.
  7. All Nippon Airways – All Nippon Airways is one of the major airlines of Japan and has always remained one of the most important Japanese airlines since it was founded nearly seventy-one years ago on 27th December in the year 1952 under the name of Japan Helicopter and Aeroplane.
  8. Cathay Pacific Airways – Cathay Pacific Airways is the Hong flag carrier and has served its nation for seventy – seven years since it was founded on 24th September in the year 1946 from its hub at the Hong Kong International Airport which is also the location of its head office.
  9. Finnair – Finnair is the Finnish flag carrier and one of the largest airlines of its native country with its headquarters and hub in Helsinki Airport. It is considered a full-service legacy airline since it was founded over a full century ago on 1st November in the year 1923 as Aero O/Y.
  10. Alaska Airlines – Alaska Airlines is one of the largest and most important airlines of the United States of America and has been repeatedly listed as one of the best airlines in the country when measured in terms of safety, security and satisfaction of the travellers.

FAQs

What constitutes air travel?

Other than aeroplanes, jet aircraft, helicopters, hot air balloons, blimps, gliders, hang gliders and parachutes are also viable means of air transport. Some of these forms are more popular than others and have specialized usage.

What is the earliest form of air travel?

Initially, flight was conducted without any human beings on board the vehicle. One of the earliest forms of air travel has been the hot air balloon which first flew in the late eighteenth century.
