How many JFK Terminals & Departures?

JFK has five active terminals (1, 4, 5, 7, 8), with each handling departures for specific airlines

Terminal 1: SkyTeam and Star Alliance international flights.

Terminal 4: Delta and many international carriers.

Terminal 5: JetBlue U.S. and some international routes.

Terminal 7: Oneworld and other global airlines.

Terminal 8: American Airlines hub and other Oneworld flights.

Pro tip: Always confirm your terminal before arriving because it affects check-in, security, and AirTrain access.
