A Guide to Seats on Frontier Airlines: Everything You Need to Know

Blog       A Guide to Seats on Frontier Airlines: Everything You Need to Know


This is an ultra-low-cost airline that provides affordable travel to passengers. You can explore destinations across the United States and beyond with this airline. Therefore, if you want to understand the seating options with this airline that can make a big difference in your travel experience, then you must take a look at the seats on Frontier Airlines and learn more about these seating features.

1. Seating Overview on Frontier Airlines

The airline uses an all-economy configuration across its fleet of Airbus A320 family aircraft. This includes the A320, A321, and A319. No doubt that the airline's focus is cost-effective flights, and you can’t expect luxurious travel or recliner-style seating. Seats at Frontier Airlines flights are designed with lightweight materials to maximize fuel efficiency.

Key features of Frontier’s seats:

The slimline design of the seats is thinner than traditional ones and offers more space for additional rows. The reclining options of standard seats do not recline, and some passengers appreciate the added personal space behind them. The tray tables are smaller behind the seats but ideal for snacks or small devices and not large enough for laptops.

2. Seat Types and Upgrades

The airline offers different types of seating options to the passengers for their preferences, and here are the types of seats that you need to know.

a) Standard Economy Seats

There are some basic seats which include the basic fare. Dimensions of the seats are 28–31 inches, with a width of about 17–18 inches. These seats are best for budget travellers who are comfortable with minimal legroom and no extras.

b) Stretch Seats

These are the premium seating options at Frontier Airlines for the people, and with these seats, you will get extra legroom and a more comfortable flying experience. Dimensions of seat pitch of 36–38 inches, these seats offer significantly more space than standard seats. These seats are recliner and located at the front of the cabin or in emergency exit rows for added convenience. These seats are good for tall passengers and families with children.

3. Seat Selection Policy

The airline operates under an à la carte pricing system. This ticket covers only the base fare, and additional services, such as seat selection, come at an extra cost.

Here, you need to understand the automatic seat assignment, and if you choose not to pay for seat selection, then the airline will assign a random seat for free during the check-in. This is ideal for solo travellers who don’t mind sitting anywhere. There is a paid seat selection as well to guarantee your preferred seat, and you can pay a fee ranging from $6 to $50

4. Family Seating Considerations

Traveling with kids? Frontier does not guarantee that families will be seated together unless they pay for seat selection. While the airline strives to accommodate families, especially those with young children, it’s a good idea to pre-select seats to ensure everyone stays together.

5. How to Choose the Best Seat

You must book the tickets early because you will get the chance to get a better selection of stretch seats and other preferred spots. You can also use the Frontier seat map and review the seat map during booking or check-in.

Summary

Hence, seats on Frontier Airlines are actually amazing for the people who are thinking of flying for the budget travel goals. These are the cost-effective choices for the people, and they can ensure easy and smooth seating with the booking of this airline.
