Do every country using 5G share these concern?

The concern of delayed flights has to be notified with all routes of flights but not with some degree of disturbance. This scenario is due to the way in which the 5G networks rolled out in countries. The range of signal emission matters with the degree of disturbance with the navigation. For example: Countries operating 5G networks with low frequency will level aircraft signals undisturbed. Because a mild or limited frequency never reaches the extends of flight ways. Whereas if the country operates a 5G network with more frequency, it ranges to the flight's routes and disturbs aircraft signal. This study analysis has confessed that reducing the frequency of 5G network signals can reduce the interference with airway travelling. Considering the results of worth studies, some countries came forward with further steps to reduce the frequency to lower the possible risk. As a part of this initiative, several countries have made decisions with framing buffer zones around airports. The 5G signals are restricted at these buffer regions, and the antennas have to be fitted downwards to prevent any major interference with the aircraft signals.

Even though these buffer zones are initiated with a very small range than other countries, and still those zones have been significantly operating at significantly higher power levels.
