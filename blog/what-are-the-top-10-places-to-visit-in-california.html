Top 10 Sights Of California

Alcatraz Island: This is the most famous island in California. It mostly gets its fame from being the place where there was the most secure federal prison in the US. This place is now a museum and open for all tourists to explore.

Universal Studios Hollywood: One of the most unique Universal Studios is located in Hollywood, California. This great amusement park is filled with a lot of exciting rides and delicious treats for you to enjoy.

Griffith Observatory: This observatory is nestled in the Hollywood Hills. One must explore this observatory to enjoy a panoramic view of the Hollywood Sign.

San Diego Zoo: This is one of the most interesting and interactive zoo experiences you can get in California. Get opportunities to even feed animals at this spot.

Golden Gate Bridge: One of the most iconic tourist spots in California is the Golden Gate Bridge. One must view this bridge when in California.

The Getty Center: This is the most astounding place to visit in Los Angeles, California. This place hosts some of the most stunning art for tourists to view.

La Jolla Cave: This is a coastline enclave in the state of California. This cave hosts one of the most dynamic views of your life that you can witness.

Disneyland Park: Enter the world of magic as you explore Disneyland Park in California. Experience the best time of your entire life here.

Palm Springs Aerial Tramway: This rotating aerial tramway ascends 8,500 feet. This tramway also showcases great panoramic views.

Monterey Bay Aquarium: This is the most stunning marine sanctuary in California. One must visit this place when in California for touring.
