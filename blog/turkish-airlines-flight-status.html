Official website of the airline

The airline's official website is turkishairlines.com. Here, you can live-track the airline's status. Once you visit the website's home page, enter the flight number to view and track the airline's live flight status online. Then, select the date of travel to view the exact details.

Select arrival airport

If you don't know the flight number, you can also view the live Turkish Airlines Flight Status by selecting the arrival airport and the date of the journey. Once you choose the arrival airport and date, all flights on this day, along with their timing and flight status, will show on the screen.

Select departure airport

Another way to view a flight's tracking on the Turkish Airlines official website is to enter the airline's departure airport. You can enter the name or airport code of the departure destination. All flights on the selected date will show on the screen.

Select flight route

These are the two methods to view the flight status online of Turkish Airlines, but the third method at the same place is selecting the flight route. This means you need to enter the departure and arrival destination and select the date.

Open the Turkish flight app

If you don't have access to the website, you can view the status of your upcoming Turkish flight through the airline's application. The app is available to download on both Android and iOS. You can view and download the application. To view the status online, you can enter the flight number, arrival or departure airport, or select the route.
