Emirates Premium Economy Cabin Sets the Gold Standard: 10/10

Blog       Emirates Premium Economy Cabin Sets the Gold Standard: 10/10


People thinking of flying with Emirates Airlines are not sure if the premium economy cabin services are in the right place, especially those who will book the Sydney to Dubai Emirates flights. Flight EK413 Premium Economy is a real-class for the travel class on an A380. This cabin class is renovated under the US$ 2 billion retrofit program. Many daily flights are available from Sydney to Dubai right now, and morning flights are the cheapest for the passengers. Summer is the peak time to travel in Europe, so almost all flights are full. No matter if it is New Year or Easter? Flights are usually entirely during the holiday and weekend time. Emirates A380 is entirely new now and You must arrive at the Sydney International Airport at least three hours early because this is a traffic-based airport, and the rush is always high. There is a dedicated premium economy check-in line, and you must show your boarding pass at the counter. People traveling in this class of Emirates flight can carry 35 kg bags, which is 15 times more than the airline's economy. Reach the security line early to avoid the last—minute hassles.

Know About Lounge

If you are an Emirates Skywards Gold Member, you have an excellent opportunity at the Sydney airport because you can access the airline's business class lounge. Suppose you think this service is only available with the first and business class. In that case, you are wrong because, with the premium economy, you can access a fantastic lounge to enjoy the unlimited buffet and drinks when you are a member of Emirates Skywards Gold Members.

Seat and cabin

Now, let’s move to the seat and cabin because seating is essential for the flyers to enjoy a comfortable journey. Seats are wow in the new premium economy of Emirates Airlines flights and look like the business class. The cabin has an ultra-luxe with wood panelling around the windows, and clean cream leather seats ensure good sleep during the journey, and space is also good. The layout of the seat is a 2-4-2 configuration, and there are two sections for each seat. Most of the seats in the premium economy cabin of Emirates are 40 inches (101.6cm) and a width of 19.5 inches (50cm). These are the recliner seats with 20cm space and have an extra 10 cm more than in the economy of Emirates. Extra leg rest is the best thing for the passengers.

Food

Now, let’s talk about the most crucial thing about airline premium economy cabin: food. Food is similar to the business class cabin. You can find many good choices in the airline's night dinner and lunch menu. If you want to eat light, you can also choose light bread.

Service

Now, let’s talk about the airline's services in this cabin class because services are tremendous and exceptional. After all, passengers get everything in the premium economy cabin before the meal, including non-alcoholic drinks and hot towels.

Amenity kit

Complimentary amenity kits are also available with the premium economy services of Emirates Airlines after take-off. These are the reusable kits, which are washable and made with kraft paper. The things you will get in the amen-premium economy amenity kit are socks, a brush, an eyeshade, and a dental kit.

The Bottom Line

Finally, we can say that the Emirates premium cabin experience is 10-10 for the passengers because all the new services and features are suitable for the passengers to enjoy a comfortable journey. You can get the feel of being in the business class at the cost of a premium economy.
