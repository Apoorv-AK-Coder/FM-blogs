Blog       10 Amazing Adventures in Massachusetts


Massachusetts is a well-known state of the United States of America and became a part of the nation on 6th February 1788. The state has remained one of the top ten smallest states in the country in terms of area. Despite this, the state has a fairly large population and ranks as the sixteenth most populated state in the country.

The state is also the third most densely populated state in the country and the most densely populated one in the New England area. The state, once dependent on agriculture, fishing and trade industries for its economy, turned itself into one of the most important manufacturing centres of the country following the Industrial Revolution.

Top season to visit Massachusetts

The best time to get cheap flights to Massachusetts is the chilling winter season. The chill in the air is quite pronounced in the state due to its latitudinal position as well as its geography. This makes this season the best time to get flights and hotel rooms in the state and to avoid the crowds that plague the peak season.

Another good time to get flight tickets to the state is the moderate and calm autumn season. The late summer season is also a good time to visit the state. During this time the state comes alive with multiple festivals making it a favourite among tourists looking to enjoy their summer holidays despite the high prices.

Top spots to visit in Massachusetts

Some of the best places in Massachusetts that you can get flight tickets to are included in the list given below along with the cities they are closest to :

Cambridge
  • The Harry Elkins Widener Memorial Library – The Harry Elkins Widener Memorial Library, locally known as the Widener Library, is a popular tourist destination of Cambridge in Massachusetts for all who love and appreciate learning.
  • The State Centre – The Stata Centre, officially known as the Ray and Maria Stata Centre is an important structure of the city designed by the famed architect, Frank Gehry for the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
Boston
  • Beacon Hill – Beacon Hill is a popular tourist spot of the capital city of the state of Massachusetts with its unique cobbled streets and preserved buildings which make it a remarkable historic structure.
  • Back Bay – Back Bay is another well-known historic neighbourhood of the city due to its beautifully preserved Victorian-style houses its location on the banks of the Charles River and the views it offers.
Worcester
  • The Antiquarian Hall – The Antiquarian Hall located in Worcester City of the Massachusetts State of United States of America is maintained by the American Antiquarian Society and preserves and displays many important historic documents of the state.
  • Bancroft Tower – The Bancroft Tower was built at the turn of the century in 1900 as an imitation of feudal castles to honour George Bancroft and is a famous building owing to its unique structure.
Springfield
  • Hampden County Memorial Bridge – The Hampden County Memorial Bridge, also known as the Springfield Memorial Bridge, is a beautiful arch bridge over the Connecticut River and a popular spot to go for a walk.
  • The Symphony Hall – The Symphony Hall is a popular concert venue in Springfield Massachusetts which was built in the early twentieth century and is a prime example of Greek Revival architecture.
Lowell
  • The City Hall Historic District – The Lowell City Hall Historic District is one of the most famous spots in the city and boasts beautiful structures such as the Lowell City Hall built in the late nineteenth century.

FAQs

What states border Massachusetts?

The state of Massachusetts is bordered by the states of Connecticut and Rhode Island to its south, the states of New Hampshire and Vermont to its north and the city of New York to its west. Additionally, the Gulf of Maine lies to the east of the state.

What is the most important airport in Massachusetts?

The most important airport in Massachusetts is the Boston Logan International Airport, also known as the General Edward Laurence Logan International Airport located jointly in East Boston and Winthrop.
