Carry-On (Hand) Baggage

You can travel with one or more items on board, depending on the travel class or ticket type, size, and weight limits. For the light-ticket holders, sometimes overhead bin space is limited, and they may be asked at no charge to check hand baggage at the gate. Thus, pack your essentials, such as medicines and valuables, in your smaller bag just in case.

Special & Oversized Baggage

There is a particular provision for special and oversized items such as sports equipment, musical instruments, wedding dresses, and more. In most cases, you’ll need to reserve space in advance and may pay an extra fee, and the airline offers dedicated provisions for items such as sports gear: skis, bicycles, golf clubs, surfboards, etc. Musical instruments include small instruments in the cabin (subject to space) or hold.

Restricted & Prohibited Items

Certain items (lithium batteries, sprays, sharp objects) are restricted in both hands and hold baggage. Always consult the “What you cannot bring” section on KLM.com before departure.

Lost, Delayed or Damaged Baggage

KLM Airlines provides immediate reporting via the airport baggage service desk if your baggage is delayed, lost, or damaged. You can also claim the compensation options & tracking via KLM’s online portal. Also, you can contact customer service for assistance with urgent re-packing essentials.

Tips for a Smooth Journey

You must measure and weigh your bags at home to avoid surprises. Sometimes, people do not weigh and measure their bags, which are overweight or oversized, at the airport. Another tip is to label each piece with your name, phone number, and itinerary. Another tip is to arrive early to allow time for check-in and security. For baggage updates, you can also check “My Trip” 24 hours before departure.

FAQs

1. What is the carry-on baggage allowance for KLM Economy Class?

Economy passengers can bring one large carry-on bag (55 × 35 × 25 cm) and one small accessory (40 × 30 × 15 cm), with a combined weight of up to 12 kg.

2. How much checked baggage can I bring on a KLM Economy Standard ticket?

Economy Standard ticket holders can check in one bag weighing up to 23 kg, with dimensions up to 158 cm (length + width + height).

3. Are there extra charges for special or oversized baggage with KLM?

KLM typically requires reservations and additional fees for special or oversized items such as sports gear, musical instruments, and wedding attire.

4. What should I do if KLM loses, delays, or damages my baggage?

Immediately report the issue at the airport baggage service desk, or manage your claim and track your baggage status online through KLM’s official portal.

5. What items are prohibited in KLM baggage?

KLM restricts lithium batteries, aerosols, sharp objects, and other hazardous materials. Always check the complete list on the airline's website before packing.

6. Does KLM provide baggage allowance for infants travelling on a lap?

Yes, infants travelling on a lap are allowed one carry-on item (55 × 35 × 25 cm, 12 kg max.) and one checked bag weighing up to 10 kg (158 cm dimensions).

7. How can I ensure a smooth baggage check-in with KLM?

To avoid issues at check-in, weigh and measure your bags in advance, label them with your contact information, and arrive early for your flight. You can verify baggage details through the “My Trip” section on KLM’s website 24 hours before departure.
