How to Use a Southwest Airlines Gift Card?

This is very easy and simple, and no hard and fast rules work here. Here are the steps to know for using the Southwest Airlines gift card. You can redeem the gift card from Southwest Airlines by following these steps.

  1. Go to the Southwest Airlines website and select the flights you want to book.
  2. Enter the passenger details as required.
  3. At checkout, enter the gift card number and PIN to apply the balance to your ticket.
  4. If the gift card balance is less than the ticket price, you can pay the remaining amount using a credit or debit card.

People can use these types of travel and flight booking gift cards to cover ticket fares, taxes, and additional fees, but they cannot be purchased for in-flight purchases, upgraded boarding, or Southwest vacation packages.

How Many Gift Cards Can You Use in One Booking?

Southwest Airlines flights allow passengers to use up to three (3) gift cards per booking. If your ticket price is higher than the total balance of the gift cards, you can pay the remaining amount using a credit or debit card. If you own more than 3 gift cards from Southwest, then you can also combine them by calling Southwest Customer Support and requesting a balance transfer onto one card.

How to Check a Southwest Airlines Gift Card Balance?

If you want to check the balance of your gift card with Southwest Airlines, then here are the steps -

Visit the Southwest Airlines website and go to the "Gift Card Balance" page.

Enter the gift card number and PIN (found on the back of the physical card or in the email for digital cards).

Click "Check Balance" to see how much is left on your card.

This is a quick and easy way to keep track of your remaining balance before booking your next flight.
