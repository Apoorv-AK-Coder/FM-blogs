Turkish airlines policies

Baggage Policies

Any baggage is allowed to be checked, but Turkish Airlines baggage policy has specific restrictions with regard to checked and cabin baggage. It depends on the route of the journey, class of travel, and type of fare. Liquids, aerosols, and gels are also prohibited in carry-on baggage, and the size of the baggage must fit the overhead storage compartments. Meanwhile, as for checked baggage, the size and number allowed also vary with domestic and international flights. Depending on the airline a passenger intends to use, passengers are encouraged to read more about baggage policies in a bid to avert additional costs.

Seating and CheckIn Policies

For a fee, passengers can choose the desired seats before purchasing the ticket or during check-in. Check-in for Turkish Airlines is also online, available 24 hours before the flight's departure and terminating 90 minutes before the flight's departure. This service allows passengers to select their seats, print their boarding passes, and, therefore, reduce the time they spend at the airports.

Turkish airlines restrictions

Size and Weight Restrictions

For Business Class, the passengers are allowed to carry two pieces of carry-on baggage. Both have to be within the airline's dimensions requirement and the total weight of both has to be less than 16kg. This considerable amount is aimed at offering the Business Class travelers some extra freedom and comfort in the course of the flight.

Prohibited Items

Turkish Airlines itself has rules on restrictions for carry-on baggage, as do most airlines, to achieve maximum safety. Prohibited items include:

Sharp objects

Prohibited carry-on items include knives, scissors, and razor blades, for instance. These, along with household and cleaning items, are seen as risks and have to be placed on hold.

Special Considerations

Personal items

They can carry one personal item on board together with the main carry-on baggage permissible by the airline. This could be a handbag, laptop bag, or a small backpack; this would be under fashion accessories and should also be marked as highly convenient. The personal item must hence be able to fit under the seat in front of you and should not hinder the movement of trolleys or inconvenience neighboring passengers.

Medical equipment

It is always important to let the airline know if you will need a specific medical device or drugs while traveling, especially if you are flying with Turkish Airlines. This advance notice enables the airline to make all the necessary preparations for your requirements as a disabled passenger so that you get your medical equipment and/or their requirements during the trip. It also assists in compliance with further needed security measures and guidelines.

Duty-free purchases

Products purchased at duty-free stores in the airport may be brought onboard in your hand-carry baggage. To meet security requirements and conform with customs regulations, such items are to be packed in their manufacturer's sealed packaging. Packaging the duty-free items in a way that they are purchased also avoids time wastage, and Boarding is made easier.

Appreciating the regulations that are part of Turkish Airlines' carry-on baggage policies will make the glorying travel a worthy deal. This will ensure that passengers are not put off by any mishaps that may occur last minute at the airport and that their traveling starts on a good note.
