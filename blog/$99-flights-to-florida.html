Hidden Gems of Florida

The state of Florida is full of surprises and wonders you dare to miss. The state offers a beautiful landscape that has no match with the rest of the world. The scenic beauty of the city during the sunrise and sun dawn is what pulls thousands of tourists daily from various corners of the world. Flight ticket bookings are mainly made during the spring seasons to make the most of the weather.

People love the cool breeze along with the tropical climate near the beaches for the ideal romantic getaway with their partner. The tourist places range from Disney World to thousands of beaches in Miami, but they are the obvious places in the state. These places remain crowded for most of the time and the fares are unnecessarily high for the huge influx. It is the hidden, offbeat places of the state that draw the main attention.

Here is a list of some of the best hidden gems in the state of Florida:

Spring Wildlife State Park

The Spring Wildlife State Park is one of their major tourist spots kept private. It houses some of the antique species which also includes one-horned rhinos. The species also includes panthers, pandas, snakes, and many more. You can also get an awesome viewpoint from the top of the mountain for a scenic beauty you can't refuse to experience. It also features an underwater observatory for more than thousands variety of fishes.

Morikami museums and Japanese garden

If you are looking for something peaceful and want to feel nature like nowhere else in the world, this is the perfect spot for you. The Museums hold some of the ancient remains of human existence along with popular peace offerings from all countries from various religions. On the other hand, the Japanese garden is the epitome of the ancient Samurai dynasty of Japan. The pagodas and the sensei take you back to the Shan-Gui dynasty of the early 1700s.

The Kampong

This is one of the finest botanical gardens that you will ever see in your life. It is filled with greenery and more than 50 varieties of plants from all around the world. It gives you the divine view of the beaches behind you along with the best tropical atmosphere on the continent. This 9-acre botanical garden is filled with coconut trees and an urban oasis. It is one of the major tourist interests of Florida, and people love to visit without fail.

Devil’s Den

It is the best place that some $99 flights can take you in Florida. It is an ancient cave that is the perfect diving spot in the entire nation. It is also the only hot spring in the city attracting a huge number of people in the winter season to have a quality time diving in warm water. You can also find wall cravings drawn from the pre-historic era, which is ideal for an archeological visit.
