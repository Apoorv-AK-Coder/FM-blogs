Must-visit Cities in Europe

It is very hard to decide on only seven cities to talk about, which would have all the soul of Europe. Still, with much consideration, these are the seven best places you cannot afford to miss-

1. Paris, France -

This is one of the most romantic cities in Europe undoubtedly. Paris is beautiful and filled with so many emotions and so much love at its every corner. Paris has one of the most sophisticated architectures and the best deserts in the whole of Europe undoubtedly.

2. London, England –

This is such a popular city that sometimes people would think the entirety of England is just this one city, London. London is everything one would imagine it to be, it's beautiful, royal, cutthroat, and very cultural. London is filled with people of all kinds of races from different parts of the world, this makes London one of the most culturally diverse.

3. Venice, Italy –

This place is on par being one of the most romantic destinations in Europe as well. Many people from around the world choose Venice as their honeymoon tourist spot. This place is so breathtakingly beautiful and serene. It is a very simple place where love is brewing at each corner.

4. Amsterdam, Netherlands –

This place although much underrated is one of the most beautiful cities to be spoken about in this list. The place is very beautiful to the point that it will take your breath away with its rich heritage and culture. The architecture that surrounds the city is not at all monotonous but is rather colorful.

5. Berlin, Germany –

This place is extremely significant in terms of modern history as we all would have known. Although that is not all to it, Berlin has seen very morbid things today date this place is completely different and is extremely beautiful. There is history etched on every brick of Berlin.

6. Greece -

This is one of the oldest historical cities there exists in Europe. The greatest of minds and the best of European mythology have originated from here. From the greatest of theatres to the most scientific astronomy everything has started right here in Greece. For every person curious about the real old world, you cannot miss exploring Greece.

7. Copenhagen, Denmark –

Denmark is a country that is known to be one of the happiest places one can choose to live in. This city has beautiful architecture, history, and so much greenery. You will feel much happier as you discover more and more of this city.

Hacking Your Europe Trip

Europe is a very complicated continent to work through. Although the cities of this continent are very beautiful each city has a different culture, language, and way of life according to their countries. So you must know of a few tricks to know when in Europe-

  1. Be Alert when you are traveling across the cities like Paris as there are just so many pickpockets and scammers. Do not give your phone to anyone to click a photo. Carry all your identity info in a fanny pack close to your body.
  2. Carry enough winter clothes. If you are traveling from countries of Asia then you would feel quite cold when exploring through these cities. So carry some winter clothes just in case.
  3. Use a translator app. Not all of Europe speaks English. So carrying a translator app will be a lifesaver for you.
  4. Calculate currency well before spending. If you do not do that then you might just spend way out of your budget and put yourself in a difficult position.
