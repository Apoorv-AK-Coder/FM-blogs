Now, you can say goodbye to the long airport lines because, with the help of the Frontier app, you can check in for your flight as early as 24 hours before the departure. Once you complete the check-in process, then, you download the digital boarding pass that can be scanned at the security checkpoints and boarding gates.

You can also manage your booking and view all upcoming trips. If you need to change a flight or want to add baggage for the selected seat, then you can choose the Frontier app, which makes it very easy to make updates on the go.

On the other hand, if you are a member of the Frontier Airlines frequent flyer program, then you can also choose the Frontier miles to pay for the services, view the points, redeem for flights and monitor the Elite Status.
