What Is Flying Blue?

This is the joint frequent flyer program of KLM and Air France, along with other partner airlines like Transavia and Kenya Airways. If you are a member of this program and you fly with KLM or any of its airline partners, then you can earn Flying Blue miles every time. You can redeem for rewards across the SkyTeam alliance.

How to Convert KLM Flights to Miles?

To earn miles, you must:

Enrol in the Flying Blue program (free of charge)

Add your Flying Blue number when booking your KLM ticket

Travel on an eligible fare and route

KLM to Miles Conversion Formula:

Flying Blue switched to a revenue-based earning system, meaning:

Miles Earned = Ticket Price (in Euros) × Earning Rate

Explorer members: 4 miles per €1 spent

Silver: 6 miles per €1

Gold: 7 miles per €1

Platinum: 8 miles per €1

Example: A €500 KLM ticket will earn you 2,000 miles as an Explorer member.

Here’s how to use KLM Flying Blue miles effectively:

  1. You can book reward flights and redeem miles for free or discounted flights on KLM and SkyTeam partners.
  2. Users can also use the promo rewards to get 25%-50% off miles needed for select routes monthly.
  3. On the other hand, if you want to upgrade to business class, then use miles to upgrade from Economy to Premium or Business Class.
  4. The other extra options which you can add to pay for checked bags, seat selection, or access to KLM Crown Lounges.
  5. KLM to Miles are also redeemable for hotel and car rental bookings, and miles can be redeemed for stays or car hires through Flying Blue partners.

People May be Asked

Flying Blue is KLM’s loyalty program. You earn miles every time you fly with KLM or its partner airlines.
