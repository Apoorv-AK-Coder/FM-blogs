Blog       Top 7 Hidden Gems in Asia


Asia would be one of the most diverse continent there exists in this world. Not only diverse in terms of culture but also the great ecology, roots, traditions, races, food everything. There are over forty-eight different countries that together would build the entirety of Asia. The greatest of minds, theories, history, civilization, and mythology have originated from these several different countries of Asia.

People from Asia have grown up to be well-competing high achievers who are running most of the top organizations of today's world. Not only that Asia is also the place where you will find some of the most welcoming and wonderful people who will welcome you with open arms into their lives.

Asia is filled with so many places which will make you fall helplessly in love with them. Some of the best places in Asia are underrated and we need more people to know about these places. Some of these best-hidden gems in Asia are-

  1. Yakushima Island, Japan- The island of magic and eternity this island beholds is beyond anyone’s imagination. This island is breathing and alive, it has a divine soul connected to it. This is one of the most underrated islands in Asia.
  2. Kanchanaburi, Thailand – This small riverside town is a two-hour drive from the capital of Thailand, Bangkok. There is something very mythical about this place that wraps you into its undeniable charm.
  3. Mu Cang Chai, Vietnam- This is a very rural town in Vietnam. Still, the vast lands filled with greenery are what will attract you the most here. There are many rural towns in Asia that will warm your heart up in the best of ways. You can enjoy many outdoor activities like paragliding here as well!
  4. Phobjikha Valley, Bhutan- This is one of the most tranquil mountain valleys. This place is rich with divinity and spiritualism owning to the several monasteries there are here. The place is very peaceful and calm and ideal place to take it slow and leave your worries.
  5. Sri Lanka- This is again a very beautiful island country which you must visit in Asia. This place has great provisions for tourists as most of its economy is based on tourism solely. There are many thrilling water adventure sports that you would be able to experience here.
  6. Taiwan- This in today’s date is a very explored and visited country even when it has so much to offer. The people and the food of Taiwan are the best things about the country. Of course, the architectural and natural beauty of the place is undeniable as well.
  7. Meghalaya, India- Although an unpopular opinion one based on real facts would be that the northeastern region of India is the most beautiful part of it. This area is unfortunately not much explored which is a shame. Meghalaya which would mean the abode of clouds has stunning ecology and warmth that encapsulates you in its beauty. You are bound to fall in love with this place once you visit it.

Things to Keep in Mind When Exploring Asia

There are many things that you should be careful about as you walk through and explore the several places in Asia. Some of these things that you must keep in mind are as the following –

  1. Get to know the culture and be respectful – Being respectful is very important when you are in Asia. Certain minor habits could be perceived as disrespectful if you are not aware of the culture of which country you visit. So please be aware and respectful.
  2. We are well dressed – Some places of Asia are more conservative them others. Dressing inappropriately in such places is a big no. So always dress modestly.
  3. Be aware of where you eat- Asian food is much different from the food that is made in other foreign countries. Not everything might suit you so beautifully careful were you eat and ask about what goes into your food.
  4. Carry a translator app or know some basic phrases- In most countries of Asia not everyone would speak English. In such cases knowing a few phrases and using a translation application would be very useful.

Airlines To Choose When In Asia

Many local airlines are significant in many countries in Asia. These airlines would allow you to enjoy the culture of Asia when you are on the flight. Three such best airlines would be-.

  1. Singapore Airlines – This airline is a flag carrier of Singapore and flies through most places across Asia. This airline has gained a great reputation for its service provided in flight.
  2. Air India - This is the flag carrier airline of India, and also the most famous operational airline here. As you travel through India and even out of it this airline would be a good one to take.
  3. All Nippon Airline- This is a famous Japanese airline which is known to be one of the best ones in the country. The service and affordability of this airline make it so significant and famous.

Conclusion

Asia has so much to offer, this list barely scratches the top of all these places. You can never stop exploring new places here it is so vast and diverse.
