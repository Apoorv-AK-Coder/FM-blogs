Hidden Gems

Aside from the popular places, many offbeat destinations will keep you awestruck. Those mesmerizing sceneries will add to your memories. United Airlines connects you to all those places that might be out of others' sight. Make sure you put these destinations on your bucket list. Here are some hidden gems which you can reach with our airlines.

Domestic destinations

  • Asheville is a city in North Carolina. Tradition and innovation blend together in this city, which features mountains, downtown, art galleries, studios, etc. It is located a few hours from the nearest international airport, Atlanta and Charlotte. You can also taste authentic cuisine from this area.
  • Santa Fe is one of the unique cities in New Mexico. This city is full of history and culture. The culture here includes Native Americans, Spanish, Mexican, modern US, and cowboy. You will get to experience these diverse cultures in this small city. The mountainous features of this city will allow you to do outdoor adventures such as hiking or mountain climbing. Also don't forget to visit the open-air market, ancient buildings, museums, galleries and restaurants.
  • Bend is a City in Oregon, situated in the valley of the Deschutes River. This city has an extinct volcano and a semi-arid climate. There are plenty of outdoor activities that you cannot miss while you are in Blend. These activities include swimming, skating, biking, fishing, hiking, rafting, and many others. There are museums and art galleries that talk about the history of this city and let tourists know about its culture.

International destinations

  • Osaka, Japan: While Tokyo and Kyoto may steal the spotlight, Osaka is a hidden gem that offers a more laid-back yet fascinating experience. Known for its street food, friendly locals, and rich history, Osaka is a city where tradition and modernity coexist in perfect harmony.
  • Ljubljana, Slovenia: Often overshadowed by its more famous neighbors, Ljubljana is a small capital city with a big heart. Its pedestrian-friendly streets, charming riverside cafes, and fairy-tale architecture make it a perfect destination for those looking for a peaceful yet vibrant European experience.
  • Cartagena, Columbia: This coastal city is a vibrant mix of old and new. With its colonial architecture, colorful streets, and lively cultural scene, Cartagena is a hidden gem that offers a rich cultural experience and stunning Caribbean beaches.
