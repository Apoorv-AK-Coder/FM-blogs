What to consider?

When looking for parking at JFK airport, convenience is the most important thing because people are looking for spots where they can get walk-on access and terminal garages. Economy and Federal offer AirTrain rides as well, and off-site parking offers shuttle pickups to travellers.

Shuttle and Train times are around 8 to 15 minutes to terminals, and off-site shuttles are also available and may take 10 to 20 minutes, depending on traffic. On the other hand, booking ahead is crucial and strongly advised, and pre-booking often provides 20-50% savings on the best spot. Generally, on-site lots are secure and reputable, and off-site lots also usually feature fencing, cameras, and lighting.
