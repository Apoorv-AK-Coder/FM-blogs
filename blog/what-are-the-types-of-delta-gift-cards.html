Types of Delta Gift Cards

As you know, you can avail of Delta gift cards online or over the phone. This proves the entire booking to be convenient. Also, travellers do not have to worry about expiration dates and they can plan their trips as they wish. They serve to be a great choice for any type of occasion hence promising you a personalised travel experience.

Since they are available in plenty of denominations, they prove to be budget-friendly and serve to be a meaningful gift. Delta Air Lines currently offers two types of gift cards. Take a look at the gift cards that you can avail for your travel-related expenses:

Traditional Gift Cards:

  1. You can avail of this gift card online. You can also avail of them at retail locations, like supermarkets or convenience stores.
  2. You will find these traditional gift cards in plenty of denominations. They range from $50 to $1,000.
  3. You can use these gift cards for purchasing Delta airfare, seat upgrades, baggage fees and other in-flight purchases.
  4. The best part is that you can redeem them online, at Delta counters, or on the phone.

eGift Cards:

  1. You can purchase electronic gift cards by visiting Delta Airline's official website. It is delivered through the email of a traveler.
  2. This mode of gift is available in plenty of denominations. Moreover to make it further special you can personalise it with a message.
  3. eGift cards are a great way to book flights, make seat upgrades, pay baggage fees and other in-flight purchases.
  4. eGift cards are flexible and can be easily stored on mobile devices, allowing you to access them easily while traveling.

Both Delta gift cards provide travellers with the flexibility to use them whenever they want to. They are available for flights to any destination hosted by Delta Air Lines, including domestic and international routes.

Why to use Delta Gift Cards

Travellers have plenty of reasons to use Delta gift cards as they have been considered to be extremely beneficial tools.

Flexibility:

Delta gift cards allow customers to choose their date and destination of travel. In simple words, with Delta gift cards travellers are promised with utmost flexibility for Delta Flight ticket bookings.

Convenient to use:

Using Delta gift cards, passengers can book their airfare, seat upgrades, baggage fees and purchase other in-flight amenities. It has been regarded to be a convenient payment option suiting various travel needs.

No expiry:

The best thing about Delta gift cards is that they do not follow any expiration dates. Hence recipients have the opportunity to use them any time they are willing to travel without worrying about their expiry.

Personalised Gift cards:

Travelers can now customize both traditional and gift cards with a message. This is a great way to make the gift card a more thoughtful option for friends, family, or close ones who love to travel.

Suited for budget travelers:

Delta gift cards have proven to be a great help for all budget travelers. They can now plan their travel expenses as early as possible by purchasing gift cards. They serve as an excellent mode of payment for future travel expenses.

Accepted everywhere:

Travellers can use Delta gift cards on numerous flights to plenty of destinations served by Delta Airlines. This also includes domestic and international routes. Hence passengers are provided a wide range of travel options.

In simple words, Delta gift cards serve as a convenient and flexible gifting option that promises 100% convenience and versatility. There are uncountable benefits to using gift cards for travel needs.
