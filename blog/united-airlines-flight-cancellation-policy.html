Blog       Full-Fledged United Airlines Flight Cancellation Policy


As we know, sometime the plan or mind may change the travelers and that’s why they are thinking to cancel the flight. If you are thinking that canceling flight is a difficult task for you then you are wrong because you can simply cancel the United Flights within few minutes. Do you know how? Take a look at the steps for how to cancel united airline flights online?

Steps For How to Cancel United Airlines Flights Online:

  • Visit On United Airlines Official Website
  • Now, Select The Option of Cancel Flight
  • At the next step, you need to confirm flight cancellation.
  • Confirm your flight cancellation
  • Your Flight Will be canceled With this Step
  • Now, you should check the availability of Future Flight Credit in Your Trip Section. If any credit is available then select the option of “Use Future Flight Credit”.
  • With this when you get back to the reservations to shop for new flights, you can simply apply the credit toward the flight.

How to Get Refund on United Flights Cancellation?

Passengers who are eligible to get a refund on the Domestic or International Flight Tickets can request a refund on the official site of united and if you are still facing issues in getting a refund in United Flight Ticket Cancellation then you can also call on United Airlines Customer Service Number. If the flights are suspended for a particular destination because of government regulatory then you will eligible to get a refund on your tickets.

Are All Tickets Changed on United Official Site?

We regret that certain United Flight Tickets cannot be changed on the official website of United or mobile application of United. (Only in the case when the ticket number does not start from 016). For the change, you need to contact the original ticketing airline agency for the changes.

How to Cancel United Flights & Rebook Later?

During, the selection of Cancel Flight Option you will also have the choice to cancel your award reservations and redeposit the miles or cancel your award reservations and get in use of those miles for another trip in the future. Here you can rebook United Flight after the cancellation.
