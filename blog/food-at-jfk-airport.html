A Simple Guide to Food at JFK Airport

Blog       A Simple Guide to Food at JFK Airport


The name of the major New York airport is John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK). There are many food options at the airport terminals, and Terminal 5 is among the most popular terminals. Terminal 5 JFK Food has been famous for its amazing quality and wide range. Flying is more memorable and stressful, especially when hungry and looking for delicious food. This blog helps you know more about the wide food options at JFK Terminal 5. At the airport, there are six active terminals with different restaurants, cafes, and grab-and-go spots. Check the simple guide to help you find something good to eat before your flight.

Main Airline for Terminal 5 at JFK Airport

Many leading airlines fly from the JFK airport, but JetBlue Airlines is the major airline at this terminal. It is a low-cost airline, and passengers who choose this airline mainly depart and arrive from Terminal 5. JFK Terminal 5 is the focus city hub of JetBlue Airlines. The primary hub has been in operation since Terminal 5 opened in 2008. Another major airline that offers services from the JFK Terminal 5 is Cape Air & Aer Lingus, which serves both domestic and international flights.

Dining Options

Terminal 5 offers a wide range of dining options and a mix of quick bites, casual cafes, and sit-down meals. There are more than 30 outlets. You can enjoy everything at this terminal, from Dunkin’ Donuts to Starbucks to World Bean Coffee. Don’t miss visiting the Horizon Bakery Café to taste the delicious French pastries and breads. Another great option to eat freshly baked products is H&H Bagels and Baked by Melissa for classic NYC flavours.

Full Service Restaurants & Bars

Before your flight, if you want to relax at the full-service restaurants and bars, enjoy the Deep Blue Sushi & Deep Blue on the Fly for Fresh sushi, and top-end sit-down experience. Another great bar is 5ive Steak, a relaxed steakhouse with bar seating.
