Blog       Delta Airlines Baggage Fees: What You Need to Know Before You Fly


Delta Airlines is an ideal example of comfort, luxury, and reliability in travel. Are you thinking of traveling with this airline but not sure about the baggage policy? Here is the Delta Airlines baggage fee, and you need to know all these things before you fly. There are different breakdowns of the baggage fee that will help you prepare for your journey.

Carry-On Allowance and Free Personal Item

The first thing is the carry-on allowance and free personal items, and Delta airline passengers must know the fact that they can bring one personal item and one carry-on item. That means you are allowed to carry one purse, laptop bag, or small backpack. One carry-on bags are also allowed, and this luggage must fit in the overhead bin space and also meet the size requirements. The size restrictions are 22 x 14 x 9 inches. These items are free in the baggage policy and do not include any additional baggage charges.

What is the checked baggage fee?

The next thing is the checked baggage fee. It totally depends on your route, class of service, and your medallion status as well. Domestic flights and international checked baggage fees in Delta flights are different. The first checked-bag fee on a domestic flight is $30, and the second checked-bag fee is $40. On the other hand, international flights baggage fee is $100

Premium Cabins Checked Baggage Fee in Delta

Another thing is the baggage fee for Delta flights in first and business class. Do you need to pay the baggage fee for the first and second bags? No, the first two bags are free. An overweight bag fee is applicable, and a checked bag fee is applicable in first and business class. The fee is $100 for bags 51–70 lbs and $200 for bags 71–100 lbs. Bags over 100 lbs are not accepted as checked baggage.

What is Oversized Baggage Fees?

If your bag exceeds the size limit of 62 linear inches, you must pay the oversized bag fee on Delta Airlines.

$200 for oversized baggage.

Bags larger than 115 linear inches are not allowed.

Special Baggage Policies

The baggage policy and terms are different for sports equipment, musical instruments, and other specialty items, but handling fees are usually charged. Musical instruments can be carried and must fit in the overhead bin or be checked as baggage.

Benefits of Delta SkyMiles Medallion Members

Here are the benefits of SkyMiles Medallion members

Silver Medallion Members: 1 free checked bag.

Gold Medallion Members and above 2 free checked bags.

These benefits can save frequent flyers significant money, especially on long trips.

Tips to Save on Baggage Fees

You can also prepay for your checked baggage online during check-in, which will help you save money. You must pack light, and you can carry a free carry-on allowance, which helps you minimize additional baggage. If you want to avail yourself of the SkyMiles program, then you can earn medallion status.

The Bottom Line

Delta Airlines baggage fees are competitive and straightforward, but preparation is key to avoiding surprises. Whether you’re traveling domestically or internationally, understanding Delta’s policies helps ensure a smooth journey. By packing smartly and utilizing loyalty programs like SkyMiles, you can reduce or even eliminate baggage costs. For the best travel experience, plan, pack efficiently, and enjoy your trip with Delta Airlines!

FAQs

What are Delta Airlines' carry-on baggage policy?

Delta Airlines allows passengers to bring one free carry-on item and one personal item, such as a purse, laptop bag, or small backpack. The carry-on bag must fit in the overhead bin and adhere to size restrictions of 22 x 14 x 9 inches.

How much is the checked baggage fee for Delta Airlines?

The checked baggage fee varies based on the route and service class:

- Domestic flights: First checked bag costs $30, and the second checked bag costs $40.

- International flights: Checked baggage fees start at $100.

Do premium cabin passengers pay baggage fees?

No, passengers in First Class and Business Class enjoy two free checked bags. However, fees apply for overweight or oversized bags:

- 51–70 lbs: $100.

- 71–100 lbs: $200.

- Bags over 100 lbs are not accepted.

What are the oversized baggage fees?

For bags exceeding 62 linear inches, Delta charges an oversized baggage fee of $200. Bags larger than 115 linear inches are not allowed as checked baggage.

Are there special policies for sports equipment and musical instruments?

Yes, sports equipment and musical instruments are subject to specific baggage policies:

- Musical instruments can be carried in the cabin if they fit in the overhead bin.

- Larger items may need to be checked and could incur handling fees.

What are the baggage benefits for SkyMiles Medallion Members?

SkyMiles Medallion Members receive the following perks:

- Silver Medallion Members: 1 free checked bag.

- Gold Medallion Members and above: 2 free checked bags.

- These benefits can lead to significant savings for frequent travelers.

How can I save on Delta Airlines baggage fees?

- Prepay for baggage during online check-in to secure lower fees.

- Pack light and take advantage of the free carry-on allowance.

- Earn Medallion Status through the SkyMiles Program for free baggage benefits.

Why is understanding Delta Airlines baggage fees important?

Knowing Delta’s baggage policies helps avoid unexpected fees and ensures a smooth travel experience. Planning ahead and packing efficiently can reduce or even eliminate baggage costs.
