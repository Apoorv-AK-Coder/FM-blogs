WestJet Airlines Class Of Services

WestJet Airlines offers impeccable classes of service to all their passengers. There are predominantly three different classes in which the services provided are divided. This airline offers different services which can be classified as the following

Economy Class

When travelling in this class of seating you will receive standard services. On booking this seat you will be provided with complimentary snacks, beverages and seats that recline well. You also get a choice of extended comfort seats in economy where you will get better legroom and more.

Premium Class

This class of seating includes better-provided services. You will get larger seats, more legroom, alcoholic beverages and hot meals on extended flights.

Business Class

This class of seating will give you the best provided in-flight service. This includes flat seats in individual pods, noise-cancelling headphones, complimentary hot meals and premium alcoholic beverages.

Processing The WestJet Check-In

Check-in can be time consuming and hectic process. Especially if you are late to the airport standing in the queue will only lead to more elevated stress levels. There is a way to make WestJet Check-In a more calm process. All you have to do is process your check-in through the online method. Check-in for WestJet flights begins online twenty-four hours before departure. Then you will get an email through which you can process your check-in online. Other than this you can also check in for this airline's flights via the official WestJet website. The benefits of you checking in online are

  1. No need to deal with long queues.
  2. You can be very efficient with time.
  3. Being able to check in at the comfort of once own surroundings.
  4. Minimal social interaction.
  5. Smoother process of checking in.
