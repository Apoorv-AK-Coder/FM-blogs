Georgetown is one of the most important cities of the country of Guyana as well as its largest city. In addition to this, the city serves as the capital of the country. The city is situated in the Demerara – Mahaica also known as Region 4 of the country on the eastern banks Demerara River where it meets the vast Atlantic Ocean.

Georgetown has been given the epithet of ‘Garden City of the Carribean’ due to the beautiful gardens and parks located within its bounds and its position on a Carribean Island. The city is located on mostly flat grounds due to its position on the coastal plains and depends on the retaining sea wall and sophisticated network of canals to keep the city from flooding.

The city is surrounded by marshy lowlands and swamps, cane fields and grassy plains stretching for great distances which add significantly to the natural beauty of the city. The city also has multiple other tourist attractions such as the St. George’s Cathedral which is a beautiful timber-painted structure built in the Gothic Revival style.

Another popular tourist destination that all travellers catching JetBlue flights to Georgetown must visit is the famed Stabroek Market, the most popular shopping complex of the city complete with an iron and steel structure and a large clock tower. As the administrative centre of the country, Georgetown also houses several of the important residences and offices of government officials.
