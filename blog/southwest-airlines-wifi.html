Air travel journey is the best time for everyone when people explore different destinations. Internet connectivity is the most important thing for travellers, and that’s why they are looking for the wifi service of the airline. Whether it is your business trip or leisure trip, it is important to stay connected to the internet all the time to send important emails or browse on social media. To fulfil all these objectives of the flyers, Southwest Airlines comes with the inflight entertainment wifi feature to make your journey memorable. This is the complete guide to understanding Southwest Airlines wifi.

How Southwest Airlines wifi Works?

The airline uses satellite-based wifi technology, which is provided by Anuvu (formerly Global Eagle). This technology allows flyers to connect to the internet while cruising at 30,000 feet. This service is available on the Southwest fleet and ensures reliable connectivity on most flights.

Wifi Cost on Southwest Airlines

Pricing is important to know, when you are getting board on Southwest airlines.

Internet Access: For just $8 per device, you can enjoy full internet access throughout your flight. This flat fee applies to domestic and international flights.

Free Messaging: Southwest offers free text messaging on all flights through apps like iMessage and WhatsApp. Connect to the wifi network and use your messaging app to stay in touch.

What Can You Do with Southwest Airlines wifi?

1. While using the Southwest Airlines wifi, you can browse the web, check and send emails, and also use social media platforms.

2. You can stream entertainment apps like Netflix, Hulu, or YouTube (subject to speed and availability).

Free Inflight Entertainment

There is no need to purchase the Southwest airlines wifi internet package because the airline complimentary provides the internet along with inflight entertainment.

You can also browse the movies and TV shows and stream a wide variety of films and shows directly to your device.

People can also access the live TV on board Southwest Airlines and access live TV channels like ESPN, CNN, and HGTV at no additional cost.

You can also track your flight with the Southwest flight track if you have access to Southwest flight wifi.

To access all these features, you have to connect with the "SouthwestWiFi" network and navigate to the entertainment portal on your device.

How to Connect to Southwest Airlines wifi?

If you want to connect to Southwest Airlines internet service on the plane, then make sure that you enable airplane mode with wifi turned on.

Now connect to the wifi network and select Southwest wifi network from your available options.

Now, open the browser and visit Southwest Airlines ' official website, which is SouthwestWiFi.com.

Choose the service and select between the free entertainment or the $8 internet plan and enjoy your inflight experience.

Limitations of Southwest Airlines wifi

There are some limitations of Southwest Airlines as well, and here are these –

Bandwidth restrictions because of the high-demand flights and these flights may experience slower speeds due to the limited bandwidth.

Southwest Airlines wifi is unavailable on short flights, and wifi may not be available on very short routes.

On the other hand, no VPN or high-bandwidth activities are one of the major reasons or limitations of Southwest Airlines wifi that you need to know.

Tips for Using Southwest Airlines wifi

First of all, you need to prepare for your device and download all required apps or offline content before your flight. You must check the aircraft availability as well because not all Southwest planes are wifi enabled, and you need to check your flight details. You must bring a backup plan, and if you require internet for work, then consider downloading the important files.
